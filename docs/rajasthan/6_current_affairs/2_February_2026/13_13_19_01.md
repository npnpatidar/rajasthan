### **10th International Day of Yoga (IDY) 2024**
*   **Date observed:** June 21, 2024.
*   **Theme:** "Yoga for Self and Society."
*   **Main Event:** Prime Minister Narendra Modi led the national celebration at the Sher-i-Kashmir International Conference Centre (SKICC) in **Srinagar, Jammu & Kashmir**.
*   **Significance:** 2024 marks the 10th anniversary of the global observance.
*   **Background:** The UN General Assembly (UNGA) declared June 21 as IDY in December 2014, following a proposal by India.

### **Inauguration of New Nalanda University Campus**
*   **Location:** Rajgir, Bihar.
*   **Inaugurated by:** PM Narendra Modi.
*   **Key Features:**
    *   **Net-Zero Campus:** Includes solar power plants, water recycling, and 100 acres of water bodies.
    *   **International Collaboration:** Established through the Nalanda University Act, 2010; involves 17 participating countries (including Australia, China, Thailand, and Laos).
*   **Historical Context:** 
    *   The original university was founded in the 5th century (Gupta Period) and flourished for 800 years.
    *   It was destroyed in the 12th century by Bakhtiyar Khilji.
