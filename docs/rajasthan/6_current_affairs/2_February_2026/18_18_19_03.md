### **50th G7 Summit: Key Highlights**

*   **Location:** Borgo Egnazia, Apulia, Italy (June 13–15, 2024).
*   **Participants:** Leaders from G7 nations (USA, UK, Canada, France, Germany, Italy, Japan) and the European Union.
*   **Special Invitees (Outreach Session):** 
    *   India (represented by PM Narendra Modi).
    *   Other nations including Brazil, Turkey, UAE, and Pope Francis (first-ever Papal participation).

#### **Major Themes & Agreements**
*   **Support for Ukraine:**
    *   Agreement to provide **$50 billion** in loans to Ukraine.
    *   Funding source: Leveraging interest earned on **frozen Russian sovereign assets** held in Western financial institutions.
*   **Economic Security & China:**
    *   Commitment to addressing "non-market policies" and industrial overcapacity in China.
    *   Focus on de-risking supply chains rather than decoupling.
*   **AI and Energy:**
    *   Discussion on "Energy and Africa/Mediterranean" and **Artificial Intelligence**.
    *   Emphasis on ethical AI development and global governance frameworks.
*   **Climate Change:**
    *   Reaffirmation of the goal to phase out unabated coal power generation during the first half of the 2030s.

#### **India’s Participation (Outreach Session)**
*   **Theme:** Focused on "Artificial Intelligence, Energy, Africa, and the Mediterranean."
*   **Key Proposals from India:**
    *   **Global South:** Emphasized the concerns and aspirations of Global South countries.
    *   **AI Leadership:** Highlighted India's "AI for All" mission and the Global Partnership on Artificial Intelligence (GPAI).
    *   **Energy Transition:** Advocated for a human-centric approach to climate change and the "Mission LiFE" initiative.

#### **G7 vs. G20 Comparison**

| Feature | G7 (Group of Seven) | G20 (Group of Twenty) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Membership** | Advanced economies only | Advanced and emerging economies |
| **Established** | 1975 | 1999 |
| **Economic Share** | ~30% of Global GDP | ~85% of Global GDP |
| **Inclusivity** | Limited; seen as a "Western club" | Broader; includes African Union (as of 2023) |

#### **Key Outcomes Table**

| Category | Action/Decision |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Infrastructure** | Promotion of the **PGII** (Partnership for Global Infrastructure and Investment) |
| **Migration** | Agreement to launch a "G7 Coalition to Prevent and Counter the Smuggling of Migrants" |
| **Food Security** | Launch of the **Apulia Food Systems Initiative** to enhance climate-resilient agriculture |
