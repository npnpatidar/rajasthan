### 5th BIMSTEC Summit
*   **Host:** Sri Lanka (held in virtual mode).
*   **Theme:** "Towards a Resilient Region, Prosperous Economies, Healthy People."
*   **BIMSTEC Charter:** The summit saw the formal adoption and signing of the BIMSTEC Charter, providing the organization with a legal identity and a formal flag/emblem.
*   **Reorganization of Pillars:** The 14 sectors of cooperation were streamlined into 7 pillars, each led by a member state:
    | Pillar | Lead Country |
    | :--- | :--- |
    | Security | India |
    | Agriculture & Food Security | Myanmar |
    | Connectivity | Thailand |
    | Science, Tech & Innovation | Sri Lanka |
    | Environment & Climate Change | Bhutan |
    | People-to-People Contact | Nepal |
    | Trade, Investment & Development | Bangladesh |
*   **Connectivity:** Adoption of the **BIMSTEC Master Plan for Transport Connectivity**, which aims to provide a framework for regional connectivity projects.

### Matua Dharma Maha Mela 2022
*   **Organizer:** All India Matua Mahasangha.
*   **Location:** Shreedham Thakurbari, Thakurnagar, West Bengal.
*   **Significance:** Celebrated to mark the **211th birth anniversary of Shree Shree Harichand Thakur**, the founder of the Matua sect.
*   **Focus:** The movement historically focused on the upliftment of the oppressed, untouchables, and backward classes through education and social reform.

### Varuna 2022 Naval Exercise
*   **Participants:** Bilateral exercise between the **Indian Navy** and the **French Navy**.
*   **Edition:** 20th edition.
*   **Location:** Arabian Sea.
*   **Objective:** To enhance interoperability between the two navies and focus on maritime security operations in the Indo-Pacific region.

    3.  Export Ecosystem
    4.  Export Performance
*   **Top Performing States:**

### Stockholm Water Prize 2022
*   **Recipient:** Professor **Wilfried Brutsaert**.
*   **Significance:** Often referred to as the "Nobel Prize of Water."
*   **Contribution:** Awarded for his work in quantifying environmental evaporation, which helps in predicting the impact of climate change on the local water cycle and water availability.
