### **International Relations & Agreements**

*   **India-Qatar Relations:**
    *   Successful release of eight former Indian Navy personnel who were previously detained in Qatar.
*   **India-UAE Strategic Cooperation:**
    *   Linkage of **UPI (India)** and **Jaywan (UAE)** payment systems to facilitate cross-border transactions.
    *   Official visit by PM Modi to Abu Dhabi to strengthen bilateral ties.
*   **India-Vietnam Defense Ties:**
    *   Signed a contract for the supply of spares for **Su-30MKI** fighter jet engines.
*   **India-Mauritius Infrastructure:**
    *   Joint inauguration of a new airstrip and a jetty at **Agalega Island** by PM Narendra Modi and Mauritius PM Pravind Jugnauth to enhance maritime security.

### **National Initiatives & Social Welfare**

*   **Skill India Mission:**
    *   India’s **1st Skill India Centre (SIC)** inaugurated in **Sambalpur, Odisha**, aimed at enhancing the employability of local youth.
*   **PM-SURAJ Portal:**
    *   Launched the **Pradhan Mantri Social Upliftment and Employment Support for Jan Kalyan (PM-SURAJ)** portal.
    *   **Objective:** To provide credit support (loans) directly to marginalized sections, including Scheduled Castes, Backward Classes, and sanitation workers.
*   **Indira Gandhi Pyari Behna Sukh Samman Nidhi Yojana (Himachal Pradesh):**
    *   Financial assistance scheme providing **₹1,500 per month** to women in the age group of 18 to 60 years.

### **Defense & Technology**

*   **Mission Divyastra (Agni-5):**
    *   Successful flight test of the **Agni-5** missile.
    *   Feature: Equipped with **Multiple Independently Targetable Re-entry Vehicle (MIRV)** technology, allowing a single missile to deliver multiple warheads to different targets.
*   **C-295 Aircraft Project:**
    *   Progress on the **'Make in India'** C-295 transport aircraft project, a collaboration between **Tata Advanced Systems** and **Airbus**.

### **Tourism & Outreach**

*   **"Chalo India" Global Campaign:**
    *   Inaugurated by the Prime Minister to tap into the Indian diaspora.
    *   **Goal:** Encouraging members of the diaspora to become brand ambassadors and invite at least five non-Indian friends to visit India.
