# PM Modi’s Visit to the UAE (February 2024)

*   **Bilateral Investment Treaty (BIT):** 
    *   India and UAE signed a BIT to significantly boost bilateral investments. 
    *   The UAE is the first country with which India has signed a BIT following the 2015-16 model treaty revisions.
*   **India-Middle East-Europe Economic Corridor (IMEC):**
    *   An Inter-Governmental Framework Agreement was signed to cooperate on the development of the IMEC.
    *   Focus areas include logistics, port connectivity, and trade facilitation.
*   **Fintech Integration:**
    *   **UPI-AANI:** Agreement to interlink India’s UPI with UAE’s instant payment platform, AANI.
    *   **RuPay-JAYWAN:** Agreement to interlink India’s RuPay cards with UAE’s JAYWAN (based on RuPay technology).
*   **Energy and Infrastructure MoUs:**
    *   MoU on Electrical Interconnection and Trade.
    *   MoU on Digital Infrastructure Projects.
    *   MoU on cooperation between the National Archives of both countries.
*   **BAPS Hindu Mandir:**
    *   Inauguration of the first traditional Hindu stone temple in Abu Dhabi.
    *   Built on 27 acres of land gifted by the UAE government.

# PRATYUSH (Probing ReionizATion of the Universe using Signal from Hydrogen)

*   **Project Overview:** A radio telescope project developed by the **Raman Research Institute (RRI)** in collaboration with **ISRO**.
*   **Objective:** To study the "Dark Ages" and "Cosmic Dawn" of the universe—the era when the first stars and galaxies were formed.
*   **Deployment:** Planned for the Moon (either in orbit or on the surface) to avoid terrestrial radio frequency interference.
*   **Significance:** It aims to detect the highly redshifted 21-cm signal from neutral hydrogen.

# International Solar Alliance (ISA)

*   **New Member:** **Malta** has officially joined the International Solar Alliance as the **119th member**.
*   **Background:** 
    *   Headquarters: Gurugram, India.
    *   Founded: Jointly launched by India and France at the COP21 Paris Climate Summit (2015).
    *   Goal: To promote solar energy deployment and reduce reliance on fossil fuels.

# MILAN 2024 Naval Exercise

*   **Edition:** 12th edition of the biennial multilateral naval exercise.
*   **Host:** Indian Navy at **Visakhapatnam** (City of Destiny).
*   **Participation:** Over 50 countries participated, featuring various warships and maritime patrol aircraft.
*   **Theme:** "Camaraderie, Cohesion, Collaboration."

# Global Pulses Convention 2024

*   **Location:** New Delhi.
*   **Organizers:** Global Pulse Confederation (GPC) and NAFED (National Agricultural Cooperative Marketing Federation of India).
*   **Key Target:** India aims to achieve **self-sufficiency in pulses by 2027**.
*   **Discussion Areas:** Enhancing production, improving supply chains, and addressing global food security.
