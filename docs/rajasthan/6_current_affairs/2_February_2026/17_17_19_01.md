### **India-Vietnam Bilateral Relations**

*   **Diplomatic Visit:** Prime Minister of Vietnam, Pham Minh Chinh, visited India (August 2024).
*   **Strategic Importance:** Vietnam is a key pillar of India’s **Act East Policy** and the Indo-Pacific Vision.
*   **Key Agreements:**
    *   Adopted a **Joint Statement** to strengthen the "Comprehensive Strategic Partnership."
    *   Finalized a **Plan of Action (2024–2028)** for implementing the partnership.
    *   **Credit Line:** India announced a **$300 million credit line** to bolster Vietnam’s maritime security.
*   **MoUs Signed:**
    *   Cooperation in customs, capacity building, and agricultural research.
    *   Vietnam joined the **Coalition for Disaster Resilient Infrastructure (CDRI)**.
    *   Inauguration of an Army Software Park at Telecommunications University, Nha Trang.

### **Project 75 (India) - Submarine Acquisition**

*   **Objective:** Procurement of **six advanced conventional submarines** for the Indian Navy.
*   **Key Technology:** Must feature **Air-Independent Propulsion (AIP)** systems to allow longer underwater endurance.
*   **Current Status:**
    *   Field Evaluation Trials (FET) have been completed.
    *   **Main Competitors:** ThyssenKrupp Marine Systems (Germany) and Navantia (Spain).
*   **Project Value:** Approximately ₹43,000 crore.

### **Political Crisis in Bangladesh**

*   **Leadership Change:** Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina resigned and fled to India following mass anti-government protests.
*   **Interim Government:** Nobel Peace Prize laureate **Muhammad Yunus** was appointed as the head of the interim government.
*   **Background:** Protests initially began against the "quota system" in government jobs but evolved into a movement for regime change.

### **National Handloom Day**

*   **Date:** Observed annually on **August 7**.
*   **Significance:** Commemorates the launch of the **Swadeshi Movement** on August 7, 1905, at Calcutta Town Hall.
*   **First Observation:** 2015 (Chennai).
*   **Objective:** To honor the handloom weaving community and highlight their contribution to India's socio-economic development.
