### **Key Highlights: Interim Union Budget 2024-25**

#### **1. Fiscal Targets and Macro-Economic Vision**
*   **Fiscal Deficit:** Target set at **5.1%** of GDP for FY25 (Revised Estimate for FY24: 5.8%).
*   **Capex Outlay:** Increased by 11.1% to **₹11,11,111 crore** (approx. 3.4% of GDP).
*   **Viksit Bharat by 2047:** Long-term vision for a "Prosperous Bharat in harmony with nature, modern infrastructure, and opportunities for all."

#### **2. Focus Groups (GYAN: Poor, Youth, Women, Farmers)**
*   **Garib (Poor):** 
    *   Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) of ₹34 lakh crore via PM-Jan Dhan accounts.
    *   25 crore people moved out of multi-dimensional poverty in the last 10 years.
*   **Yuva (Youth):** 
    *   Skill India Mission trained 1.4 crore youth and upskilled/reskilled 54 lakh.
    *   PM Mudra Yojana sanctioned 43 crore loans for entrepreneurship.
*   **Annadata (Farmers):** 
    *   PM-KISAN provided direct financial assistance to 11.8 crore farmers.
    *   PM Fasal Bima Yojana provided crop insurance to 4 crore farmers.
*   **Nari Shakti (Women):** 
    *   30 crore Mudra Yojana loans provided to women entrepreneurs.
    *   Target for 'Lakhpati Didi' increased from 2 crore to 3 crore.

#### **3. Infrastructure and Connectivity**
*   **Railway Corridors:** Implementation of three major economic railway corridor programs:
    1.  Energy, Mineral, and Cement corridor.
    2.  Port connectivity corridor.
    3.  High-traffic density corridor.
*   **Bogi Conversion:** 40,000 normal rail bogies to be converted to **Vande Bharat standards**.
*   **Aviation:** Expansion of existing airports and development of new ones under the UDAN scheme.

#### **4. Energy and Environment**
*   **PM-Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana:** 
    *   1 crore households to obtain 300 units of free electricity monthly through rooftop solarization.
    *   Expected savings of ₹15,000–₹18,000 per household annually.
*   **Green Energy:** 
    *   Viability Gap Funding (VGF) for 1 GW offshore wind energy.
    *   Phased mandatory blending of Compressed Biogas (CBG) in CNG and PNG.

#### **5. Technology and Innovation**
*   **Innovation Fund:** A corpus of **₹1 lakh crore** established with 50-year interest-free loans to provide long-term financing for R&D in sunrise sectors.
*   **Deep-Tech:** New scheme launched to strengthen deep-tech technologies for defense purposes and self-reliance (*Aatmanirbharta*).

#### **6. Health and Housing**
*   **Cervical Cancer:** Government to encourage vaccination for girls aged 9–14 years.
*   **U-WIN Platform:** To be rolled out for managing immunization efforts and Intensified Mission Indradhanush.
*   **Housing:** Under PM Awas Yojana (Gramin), 2 crore additional houses to be built in the next 5 years to meet increased demand.

#### **7. Taxation**
| Category | Change |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Direct Tax Rates** | No change. |
| **Indirect Tax Rates** | No change. |
| **Startup Tax Benefits** | Extended until March 31, 2025. |
| **Outstanding Tax Demands** | Withdrawal of demands up to ₹25,000 (pre-2010) and ₹10,000 (2010-2015). |
