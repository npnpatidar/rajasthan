### Rajasthan Jan Vishwas Ordinance 2025

*   The Rajasthan Cabinet approved the Rajasthan Jan Vishwas Ordinance 2025 on December 3, 2025.
*   **Objective:** To improve the 'Ease of Doing Business' and 'Ease of Living'.
*   The ordinance replaces imprisonment with fines for minor and technical offenses.
*   Criminal penalties have been removed from 11 acts.
*   **Key Acts Affected:**
    *   Rajasthan Forest Act 1953 (e.g., cattle grazing in forests will now incur a fine instead of imprisonment).
    *   Industries Assistance Act 1961.
    *   Jaipur Water Supply and Sewerage Board Act 2018 (e.g., water misuse or illegal connections will now incur a fine instead of imprisonment).
*   The ordinance is based on the Central Government's Jan Vishwas Act 2023.

### Kumbalgarh Festival

*   The 19th edition of the Kumbalgarh Festival was held in Rajsamand district from December 1 to 3, 2025.
*   **Historical Context (of Kumbalgarh Fort):**
    *   Located on Hemkoot hills.
    *   Built by Rana Kumbha.
    *   Features a 36 km long wall, the second longest in the world.
    *   Abul Fazal remarked that looking up at it makes one's turban fall off.
    *   Houses Katargarh Fort, known as the "Eye of Mewar."
    *   Maharana Pratap was born here.

### Khel Rajasthan Games 2026

*   The Rajasthan Government announced in Budget 2024-25 that it will organize "Khel Rajasthan Games" in 2026.
*   These games will be held at Panchayat, Block, District, and State levels, mirroring the "Khelo India Games" model.
*   Tentatively scheduled for January 2026.

### Pravasi Rajasthani Divas

*   The first-ever Pravasi Rajasthani program will be held on December 10, 2025.
*   33 prominent personalities of Rajasthani origin from around the world will attend as state guests.

### Rajasthan Players in Indian Basketball Team
