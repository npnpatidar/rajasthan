### Geopolitics and International Relations

*   **South China Sea (SCS) Developments:** Increased naval maneuvers by certain littoral states following the recent deployment of UNCLOS-mandated monitoring vessels. The 2016 Permanent Court of Arbitration (PCA) ruling regarding the Nine-Dash Line remains a point of diplomatic contention.
*   **G20 Presidency Outcomes:** Key focus areas during the recent presidency included the reform of multilateral development banks (MDBs) and achieving consensus on a framework for global debt resolution, particularly impacting low-income countries.
*   **India-EFTA Trade Negotiations:** Progress noted in the talks for a Trade and Economic Partnership Agreement (TEPA) with the European Free Trade Association bloc (Iceland, Liechtenstein, Norway, Switzerland).
*   **Minamata Convention:** India reaffirmed its commitment to the global treaty targeting mercury emissions and discharge, emphasizing phase-out deadlines for industrial processes using mercury.

### Domestic Policy and Economics

| Policy/Scheme | Objective | Key Target/Data Point |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **National Logistics Policy (NLP)** | Reduction of logistics costs as a percentage of GDP. | Target: Lowering costs from 14% to single digits (approx. 8%) by 2030. |
| **PM-PRANAM Scheme** | Promotion of balanced fertilizer use and reduction of chemical fertilizer subsidy burden. | Encouraging use of alternative fertilizers and bio-inputs. |
| **RBI Monetary Policy** | Maintained the repo rate at 6.5%. | Rationale cited: Need to anchor inflation expectations, particularly concerning sticky core inflation. |
| **Foreign Trade Policy 2023** | Focus on promoting trade settlement in the Indian Rupee (INR). | Aimed at globalizing the INR and minimizing exchange rate volatility for exporters and importers. |

### Science, Technology, and Governance

*   **Space Program Success:** ISRO successfully launched the Chandrayaan-3 mission, achieving a soft landing near the lunar south pole region.
*   **Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023:** Legislation enacted establishing duties for 'Data Fiduciaries' and recognizing specific rights for 'Data Principals' (individuals). Imposes significant penalties for data breaches.
*   **Green Hydrogen Mission:** Allocation of funds toward scaling up green hydrogen production capacity and establishing export hubs for green ammonia. Target is to achieve 5 MMT (Million Metric Tons) production by 2030.
