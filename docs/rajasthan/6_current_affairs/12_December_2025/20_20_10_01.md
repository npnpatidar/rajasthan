**Rajasthan Current Affairs - December 20, 2025**

**State-Level Women's Conferences in Rajasthan**

*   **Dholpur Conference:**
    *   Held on December 19, 2025, in Panchgaon, Dholpur, to mark the completion of the Rajasthan government's two-year tenure.
    *   ₹154 crore was distributed under social pension schemes.
    *   ₹100 crore in loans was disbursed to women associated with Rajivika.
*   **Previous Conferences:**
    *   **Udaipur Conference (One-Year Tenure):**
        *   Held on December 14, 2024, in Udaipur, marking the government's one-year tenure.
        *   Chief Minister Amrit Aahar Yojana was launched.
        *   Aimed to establish five Anganwadi centers in each assembly constituency and one 'Adarsh Anganwadi' in each block.
    *   **Barmer Conference (Rajasthan Day):**
        *   Held on March 25, 2025, at Adarsh Stadium, Barmer, as part of Rajasthan Day celebrations (March 25-31, 2025).
        *   5,000 girl students received scooters.

**Eat Right School Program**

*   The "Eat Right School" program is being implemented in Rajasthan.
*   It operates under the state's "Niramay Rajasthan Abhiyan" and is run by the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI).
*   The first phase targets 10,000 schools.
*   **Niramay Rajasthan Abhiyan:**
    *   Launched on April 7, 2025.
    *   **Key Initiatives:**
        *   **Chief Minister Ayushman Adarsh Gram Panchayat Abhiyan:** Rewards panchayats with ₹11 lakh for achieving 100% on nine health standards.
        *   **Mission Madhuhari:** Focuses on diabetes patients.
        *   **Mission Liver Smile.**
        *   **Eat Right Rajasthan Abhiyan.**
*   **Eat Right India Program:** A pan-India initiative by FSSAI to ensure quality food. Examples include certified "Eat Right" railway stations (Jaipur, Ajmer, Udaipur) and places like Mansarovar Chaupati.

**Rajasthan International Film Festival (RIFF)**

*   The 12th edition of the Rajasthan International Film Festival (RIFF) will be held from January 31 to February 4, 2026, in Jodhpur.
*   It will commence at Miraj Bioscope and conclude at Mehrangarh Fort.
*   **Past Editions/Related Festivals:**
    *   **11th RIFF:** Held in February 2025 in Jodhpur.
    *   **International Film Festival Award (IFAA) Program:** Held in March 2025 in Jaipur, marking its first time in Rajasthan.
    *   **Rajasthan Film Festival:** Held in September 2025 in Jaipur.
    *   **Jaipur Film Festival:** Held in January 2025 in Jaipur.

**University Collaborations for Employment-Oriented Education**

*   Jay Narayan Vyas University (JNVU), Jodhpur, has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) with the Board of Apprenticeship Training (under the Central Ministry of Education).
*   The MOU aims to make higher education more employment-oriented.
*   **About JNVU Jodhpur:**
    *   Established in 1962.
    *   Hosts the Tribal Study Center and Veer Teja Ji Study Center.
*   **Other Noted Universities:**
    *   **M.B.M. University, Jodhpur:** Rajasthan's third technical university. Developed blockchain technology for home voting and a technique to produce ink from vehicle exhaust.
    *   **Swami Keshavanand Rajasthan Agricultural University:** Rajasthan's first agricultural university.
    *   **Mohanlal Sukhadia University (MLSU), Udaipur:** Established in 1962. It was the first university to establish a Constitution Park and hosts the Sant Pipa Study Center.

**IPL 2026 Mini-Auction**
