### Environment & International Summits

*   **UN Environment Assembly (UNEA-7)**
    *   Seventh session (UNEA-7) held from December 8-12.
    *   Location: Nairobi, Kenya (headquarters of UNEP).
    *   Theme: "Promoting Sustainable Solutions for a Stronger Planet."
    *   First UNEA session occurred in 2014.
    *   India's representation: Kirtivardhan Singh (Minister of State for Environment).
    *   UNEP (United Nations Environment Programme) established on June 5, 1972.
*   **Traditional Medicine Global Summit**
    *   Second global summit on traditional medicine.
    *   Host: World Health Organization (WHO).
    *   Co-host: India (in Delhi).
*   **World Summit on Disaster Management**
    *   Location: Dehradun, Uttarakhand.
    *   Inaugurated by: Dr. Jitendra Singh (Union Minister of State for Science & Technology).
    *   Three new weather radars for real-time monitoring installed in Uttarakhand: Pant Nagar, Auli, and Haridwar.
    *   Dehradun is home to the Indian Forest Survey Report, published biannually since 1987.

### Governance & Policy

*   **100% Voter Roll Digitalization (SIR)**
    *   Rajasthan became the first state to achieve 100% digitalization of voter rolls under the Special Intensive Revision (SIR) program.
    *   SIR was initiated by the Election Commission of India to update voter lists, remove deceased voters, and prevent fraudulent voting.
    *   The program first started in Bihar.
    *   Booth Level Officers (BLOs) are responsible for implementing SIR.
    *   Rajasthan digitized 5.46 crore voter rolls.
*   **Supreme Court Directive on Women's Reservation in State Bar Councils**
    *   The Supreme Court directed State Bar Councils to implement a 30% reservation for women.
    *   State Bar Councils were established under the Advocates Act, 1961.
    *   The Nari Vandan Adhiniyam (106th Constitutional Amendment) provides 1/3rd reservation for women in the Lok Sabha and Vidhan Sabhas.
    *   Justice Fatima Bibi was the first female judge of the Supreme Court. Anna Chandy (Kerala) was the first female judge of a High Court, and Leila Seth (Himachal Pradesh) was the first female Chief Justice of a High Court.
*   **Corporate Average Fuel Efficiency (CAFE) Rules**
    *   Issued by the Government of India.
    *   Aimed at improving fuel efficiency, especially in the context of competition in the small car market (e.g., between Maruti Suzuki and Tata Motors).
*   **Abhay Brigade (Bihar)**
    *   Launched by Bihar to enhance women's safety, improve public place security, and curb hooliganism.
