**Health Targets and Initiatives**
*   **Tuberculosis (TB):**
    *   India's target for TB elimination: 2025 ("TB Harega Desh Jeetega").
    *   Nikhsay Portal launched for TB.
*   **Leprosy:** Jordan recently declared free of leprosy.
*   **Disease-Free Status (India):** India has become free of Polio, Smallpox, and Trachoma.
*   **Sickle Cell Anemia:** India's target for Sickle Cell Anemia elimination: 2047.
*   **Minamata Disease:** Caused by excess mercury in water.
*   **Itai-Itai Disease:** Caused by excess cadmium.
*   **Blue Baby Syndrome:** Caused by excess nitrates.
*   **Silicosis:** Affects miners.
*   **Cancer:**
    *   World Cancer Day: February 4.
    *   Cobalt-60 commonly used in cancer treatment.
    *   Iodine-131 used for thyroid cancer.

**Government and Public Administration (India)**
*   **GST Council:**
    *   Established on recommendation of Vijay Kelkar Committee.
    *   Act: 101st Constitutional Amendment Act, 2016, implemented July 1, 2017.
    *   Current Rates: 5%, 18%, 40% (referred to as Next Generation GST Reforms).
    *   Head: Union Finance Minister.
*   **National Council for Educational Research and Training (NCERT):**
    *   Established: 1961. Headquarters: New Delhi. Head: Dinesh Saklani. Status: Deemed University.
*   **University Grants Commission (UGC):**
    *   Established: 1956 by Maulana Azad. Headquarters: Delhi.
*   **Archaeological Survey of India (ASI):**
    *   Established: 1861 by Alexander Cunningham.
*   **Indian Olympic Association (IOA):**
    *   Established: 1927 by Sir Dorabji Tata. Headquarters: Delhi. Current Head: PT Usha (first female head).
*   **Indian Judiciary:**
    *   Opposition in Parliament moved a proposal to remove Justice G.R. Swaminathan of the Madras High Court.
    *   Grounds for removal of a judge: Misbehaviour or Incapacity (Article 124 of the Constitution).
    *   Process: Requires 100 MPs in Lok Sabha or 50 MPs in Rajya Sabha to sign, then passed by special majority in both houses before Presidential removal order.

**International Affairs**
*   **Austria:** Banned headscarves in schools for girls under 14, citing empowerment of girls and their rights.
*   **Israel-Gaza Conflict:**
    *   Israeli military declared the "Yellow Line" as the new border between Israel and Gaza, implying 58% of the Gaza Strip's area would come under Israeli control.
    *   A 20-point peace plan was launched by Donald Trump to establish peace between Israel and Hamas.
    *   Jerusalem is a disputed holy site for Islam and Judaism.
*   **Interpol Blue Notice (Goa Incident):**
    *   Interpol issued a Blue Notice against owners of a Goa nightclub involved in a fire incident, leading to their arrest in Phuket, Thailand.
    *   **Blue Notice:** Issued to identify or obtain information about a person's location.
    *   **Red Notice:** For wanted persons/terrorists.
    *   **Black Notice:** For unidentified bodies.
    *   **Yellow Notice:** For missing persons.
    *   Interpol established: September 7, 1923. Headquarters: Lyon, France.
*   **India-Sweden Collaboration:** Launched seven Green Tech projects to decarbonize steel and cement sectors. India's net-zero carbon emission target: 2070.
