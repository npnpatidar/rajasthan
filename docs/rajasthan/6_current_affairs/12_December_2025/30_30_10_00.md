**International Developments and Security**

*   **Gaza Conflict/Aid:** The UN Security Council passed Resolution 2720 concerning aid distribution in the Gaza strip. The resolution calls for an immediate scaling up of humanitarian assistance.
*   **UN Appointments:** António Guterres continues to serve as the UN Secretary-General.

**Global Climate Policy**

*   **COP28 (Dubai):** The conference concluded with the adoption of the ‘UAE Consensus.’
*   **Fossil Fuels:** The ‘UAE Consensus’ is significant as it includes, for the first time, explicit language mentioning the need for countries to 'transition away' from fossil fuels.

**Domestic Economy (India)**

*   **GDP Growth:** The GDP growth rate for Q3 FY24 was reported at 8.4%.
*   **Growth Drivers:** This economic growth was primarily attributed to strong performance in the manufacturing and construction sectors.
