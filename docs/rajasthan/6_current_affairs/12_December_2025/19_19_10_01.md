### Rajasthan Current Affairs - December 2025

#### Key Appointments and Education
*   **Swami Keshwanand Rajasthan Agricultural University (Bikaner):** Dr. Rajendra Babu Dubey appointed as Kulaguru (Vice-Chancellor).
    *   **Rajasthan Agricultural Universities:**
        *   **Swami Keshwanand A.U. (Bikaner):** Rajasthan's first Agricultural University.
        *   **Jodhpur A.U. (Jodhpur):** Awarded "Best Research Institute," features a Constitution Park, publishes "Marudhara" magazine, developed "Jodhpur Jeera One" (cumin) and "Rajgira 0.2" (nutritious grain), houses the Namo Didi Training Center.
        *   **Kota A.U. (Kota):** Agricultural University.
        *   **Maharana Pratap University of Agriculture and Technology (MPUAT, Udaipur):** Developed "Chetak" (opium) and "Pratap Shankar Six" (maize) varieties, launched an FM Radio (90.4), hosts Rajasthan's first Fishery and Dairy College.
        *   **Shri Karan Narendra Agricultural University (Jobner, Jaipur):** Houses Rajasthan's second Veterinary University, developed "Karan Shankar Chana" (gram) and "RF 290" (fennel) varieties.
    *   **Kulaguru Concept:** Introduced in Budget 2024-25, replacing "Kulpati" (Vice-Chancellor). First appointment was Akhil Ranjan Garg for Bikaner Technical University.
*   **Higher Education and Technology Hub:** Triple IT Kota (Indian Institute of Information Technology) will be developed as a Higher Education and Technology Hub.

#### Judicial and Legal Updates
*   **West Zone Regional Conference:** National Judicial Academy organized the conference in Jaisalmer on December 13-14, 2025.
    *   Organized by Rajasthan High Court and Rajasthan State Judicial Academy (both located in Jodhpur).
    *   Rajasthan High Court: Inaugurated on August 29, 1949, by Maharaja Sawai Man Singh. First Chief Justice was Kamal Kant Verma. Current Acting Chief Justice is Sanjeev Prakash Sharma (succeeded K.R. Shriram).
    *   Inaugurated by Chief Justice of India, Surya Kant.
*   **Shops and Commercial Establishments (Amendment) Ordinance 2025:** Notification issued on December 18, 2025.
    *   **Minor Age:** Increased from 12 to 14 years for working in shops/establishments.
    *   **Night Work:** Children aged 14-18 years are prohibited from working at night.
    *   **Daily Work Hours:** Increased from 9 to 10 hours.
    *   **Overtime Limit:** Quarterly overtime increased from 125 hours to 144 hours.

#### Festivals and Environment
*   **Kurja Mahotsav 2025:** To be held in Khichan village, Phalodi, from December 26-27, 2025.
*   **First Center of Excellence for Climate Change Adaptation:** To be established in Jagatpura, Jaipur, at a cost of ₹150 crore.
    *   Announced in Budget 2024-25, which was termed the "Green Budget."
    *   **Other Green Budget Initiatives:**
        *   Harit Aravalli Development Project (₹250 crore).
        *   "Waste to Wealth Parks" to be developed at all district headquarters.
        *   Annual assistance of ₹5,000 for small and marginal farmers using bullock-based farming.
        *   Ghariyal Rearing Center to be established at Pali Ghat, Sawai Madhopur.
        *   Leopard Reserve Areas to be developed at Amrak Mahadev Reserve (Udaipur) and Ganga Bhairav Ghati Reserve (Ajmer).
