### Current Affairs: December 17, 2025

#### Prabal Program and Education Initiatives
*   **State-level Prabal Program:** Organized at the Rajasthan Legislative Assembly, Jaipur.
*   **Nodal Agency:** Rajasthan School Education Council (formed September 3, 2020), under the School Education Department.
*   **Prabal Program Objective:** Skill development for students of classes 9-12 as per 21st-century requirements.
*   **Prabal Program Launch:** June 2025.
*   **Rajasthan Yuva Sabha:** Held on December 15, 2025, at the Rajasthan Legislative Assembly as part of the Prabal Program.
*   **Shikshit Rajasthan Abhiyan (Educated Rajasthan Campaign):** Launched March 29, 2025, from Kota.
*   **Prakhar Rajasthan Abhiyan:** Part of Shikshit Rajasthan Abhiyan, ran from September 5 to December 5, 2025, focusing on reading skill development for students of classes 3-8.

#### Archaeological Discoveries
*   **Jasanagar, Nagaur:** 3500-year-old rare coin-making seals (stamps) and 16 cowries found on the banks of the Luni River.
*   **Other Recent Discoveries in Rajasthan:**
    *   **Jhunjhunu:** Bansiyal and Rampura (approx. 4500 years old).
    *   **Deeg:** Behaj village.
    *   **Jaisalmer:** Ratadia ki Beri (approx. 4500 years old, Harappan/Indus Valley Civilization remains).
    *   **Jaisalmer:** Sadehwala area (near Ramgarh) also yielded Harappan/Indus Valley Civilization remains. (Sadehwala, Tanot, Ghotaru, Manihari Tiba are also natural gas reserves).

#### Operation Sindoor
*   **Honoree:** ADG Biju George Joseph, for outstanding work in Operation Sindoor.
*   **Context:** Operation Sindoor was a "Sashastra Air Strike" by the Indian Air Force on May 6-7, 2025, in response to a Pakistani terrorist attack in Pahalgam (April 2025).
*   **Related Exercise:** Operation Shield (a mock drill) was conducted on May 31, 2025, as part of Operation Sindoor.
*   **Key Military Assets:** BrahMos and Akash missiles, Akash Teer and Sudarshan Chakra (S-400) air defense systems.

#### Watershed Festival 2025
*   **State-level launch:** Undwa village, Kota, on November 13, 2025.
*   **Inaugurator:** Madan Dilawar.
*   **Context:** Part of the Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana (PMKSY – Prime Minister’s Agricultural Irrigation Scheme), a flagship scheme in Rajasthan.
*   **PMKSY Details:**
    *   **Tagline:** "Har Khet ko Pani" (Water to every field).
    *   **Launch:** July 1, 2015.
    *   **Objective:** Promote modern irrigation equipment (e.g., drip/sprinkler systems) for water conservation.
    *   **Funding (for irrigation equipment subsidy):** 60% Centre, 40% State (90:10 in Himalayan regions).
    *   **Subsidies for Farmers (on irrigation equipment purchase):**
        *   Small/Marginal Farmers: 55%
        *   Other Farmers: 45%
*   **Other notable mentions:**
    *   **Kishangarh & Menar:** Declared Ramsar Sites on June 4, 2025.
    *   **Devmali, Beawar:** Awarded "Best Tourism Village."

#### Mountaineering Achievements
*   **Mount Vinson Massif:** Manasvi Agarwal (from Udaipur) hoisted the Indian flag on Mount Vinson Massif, Antarctica's highest peak.
*   **Other Notable Women from Rajasthan in Mountaineering/Sports:**
    *   **Captain Shiva Chauhan (Udaipur):** First woman deployed at the highest army post (Siachen).
    *   **Gaarvi Singhvi (Udaipur):** Youngest to cross the English Channel.
    *   **Bhakti Sharma (Udaipur):** First woman from Rajasthan to cross the English Channel.
    *   **Dholi Meena (Dausa):** Hoisted flag on Mount Etna.
    *   **Geeta Samota (Sikar):** First female CISF officer to climb Mount Everest.
    *   **Deepika Rathore (Didwana):** Climbed Mount Everest twice, hoisted flag on Kilimanjaro.
