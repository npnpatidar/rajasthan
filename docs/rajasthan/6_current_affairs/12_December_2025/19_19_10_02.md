## Current Affairs Summary

### Global and National Reports/Indices
*   **WADA's 2024 Testing Figures Report:** India topped the list for anti-doping rule violations for the third consecutive year (2022, 2023, 2024). In 2024, 260 Indian athletes tested positive (highest globally). WADA was established in 1999 with headquarters in Montreal, Canada.
*   **Makhana (Fox Nut) Production:** India is the world's largest producer, accounting for 80% of global production. Bihar is India's largest producer (85% of national production), with Darbhanga as a key center. The National Makhana Board was established on September 15, 2025, in Purnia, Bihar.
*   **Times Higher Education World University Rankings:** Indian Institute of Science, Bengaluru, is the top Indian institute.

### Awards and Honors
*   **PM Modi's International Awards:** Received the "Great Honor of Nishan" from Ethiopia and the "Order of Oman" (Oman's highest civilian award) from Sultan Haitham bin Tariq.
*   **CEAT Cricket Lifetime Achievement Award 2025:** Awarded to Brian Lara (West Indies) and Bhagwat Chandrasekhar (India).
*   **Hornbill Festival:** Nagaland's festival received the Silver Banyan Award for cultural music and dance.

### International Relations and Events
*   **PM Modi's Foreign Visits:** Visited Jordan, Ethiopia, and Oman.
*   **India-Oman Relations:** A Comprehensive Economic Partnership Agreement (CEPA) was signed to boost trade. India constructed the Duqm Port in Oman.
*   **PM Modi's Tribute in Ethiopia:** Paid respects at the Adwa Victory Memorial in Addis Ababa, commemorating Ethiopia's victory over Italy in the 1896 Battle of Adwa.
*   **New York Declaration:** Related to a peaceful resolution of the Israel-Palestine issue.
*   **SCO Summit 2027:** Pakistan will host the summit (Tianjin, China, is the host for 2025).
*   **CIA Nuclear Device in Himalayas:** US intelligence agency CIA reportedly lost a nuclear device in the Himalayas 60 years ago (around 1965), a revelation made now.
