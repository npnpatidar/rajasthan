**Gagron Fort Painting Festival**

*   At the Gagron Fort Painting Festival in Jhalawar, 5100 students created a world record by painting simultaneously. This achievement was registered in the World Book of Records.
*   The initiative was led by District Collector Ajay Singh Rathore.
*   Jhalawar District Profile (Panchgaurav):
    *   **Agriculture:** Orange
    *   **Vegetation:** Teakwood
    *   **Industry:** Kota Stone
    *   **Tourism:** Gagron Fort
    *   **Sport:** Basketball
*   Gagron Fort:
    *   A water fort located at the confluence of the Kali Sindh and Ahu rivers.
    *   Construction attributed to Bijaldev in the 11th century.
    *   Houses the dargah of Meethe Shah and the cenotaph of Saint Pipa.
    *   Site of the Kota Riyasat's mint (Taksaal).
*   Ghoomar Festival: Held on November 19, 2025, across all divisional headquarters in Rajasthan. A record was set with over 6100 women performing Ghoomar simultaneously.
