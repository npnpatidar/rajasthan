**Medical Advancements in Rajasthan**
*   **First Ex-Utero Intrapartum Treatment (EXIT) Surgery:** Performed successfully on a fetus in the womb by All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), Jodhpur.
    *   **AIIMS Jodhpur:**
        *   Being developed as a Medical Hub/Park.
        *   Houses an Animal House for research on zoonotic diseases.
        *   Performed the state's first robotic surgery.
        *   Provided medical facilities via drone in Abu Road, Sirohi, with technical support from IIT Jodhpur.
*   **Dr. Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan Rajasthan Ayurveda University, Jodhpur:**
    *   The second Ayurveda university in the country and the first in the state.
    *   Established the International Center of Excellence in Panchkarma.
*   **Sawai Man Singh (SMS) Hospital, Jaipur:**
    *   Houses the state's first Plasma Bank.
    *   Initiated the state's first Air Ambulance service.
    *   Performed the state's first Heart Transplant.
    *   Home to the Yoga and Ayurveda Research Institute.
    *   Launched the state's first Robotic Lab.
    *   Features a Sports Injury Center.
    *   Honored for organ donation promotion.
    *   Houses the State Cancer Institute.
*   **RIMS (Rajasthan Institute of Medical Sciences):** Being developed on the lines of AIIMS at Rajasthan University of Health Sciences (RUHS).
