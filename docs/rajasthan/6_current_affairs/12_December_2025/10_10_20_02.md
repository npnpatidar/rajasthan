**Pravasi Rajasthani Divas**

*   **Event:** First Pravasi Rajasthani Divas (Non-Resident Rajasthani Day)
*   **Date:** December 10, 2025
*   **Location:** Jaipur Exhibition and Convention Center (JECCC), Sitapura, Jaipur
*   **Background (Rising Rajasthan Program - Dec 9-11, 2024):**
    *   **December 9, 2024:** Inaugurated by PM Narendra Modi, who received a sandalwood sword crafted by Vinod Jangid (Churu).
    *   **December 10, 2024:** Rajasthan CM announced:
        *   Rajasthan's GDP aims to reach $350 billion in the next 5 years.
        *   Pravasi Rajasthani Divas to be organized annually on December 10.
*   **Purpose:** To connect Non-Resident Rajasthanis with the state to utilize their energy, knowledge, and businesses for Rajasthan's development and local employment.
*   **Related Initiatives:**
    *   Pravasi Rajasthani Policy was unveiled.
    *   Pravasi Rajasthani Meets were held:
        *   Hyderabad: September 26, 2025
        *   Surat: October 8, 2025
        *   Kolkata: October 28, 2025
*   **Key Guests (Dec 10, 2025 event):** Gulab Chand Kataria (Governor of Punjab), Bhajan Lal Sharma, Bhupendra Yadav (Union Minister for Environment, Forests & Climate Change, MP from Alwar), Piyush Goyal, and 33 state guests.

**Voter List Special Intensive Review Program**

*   **Program:** Special Intensive Review Program for Voter List Digitization.
*   **Initiator:** Election Commission of India.
*   **Announcement:** October 2025, for 12 states.
*   **Process:** Booth Level Officers (BLOs) conducted door-to-door verification and data collection.
*   **Dates:** November 4 - December 4, for data collection and digitization.
*   **Rajasthan's Achievement:** Rajasthan is the *first state* in India to achieve 100% digitization in this program.
*   **Timeline for Voter List Update:**
    *   Draft voter list: December 16.
    *   Deadline for raising objections: January 15.
    *   Final voter list: February 7.
