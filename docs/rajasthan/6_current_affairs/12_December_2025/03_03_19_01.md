**Rajasthan Current Affairs - December 3, 2024**

**Mineral Discoveries in Rajasthan**
*   **Banswara:** Discovery of the state's only graphite belt, specifically in the Dukwara area (between Ganoda and Ghatol). Graphite is categorized as a Critical Mineral.
    *   **Other recent discoveries:**
        *   **Karoli:** Iron ore deposits.
        *   **Balotra (Siwana, Bhati village):** Rare Earth Metals.
        *   **Nagaur (Degana):** Lithium deposits.
        *   **Bikaner, Churu, Hanumangarh:** Potash deposits.
        *   **Banswara (Ghatol block, Kakariya area):** Gold deposits.

**Renaming of Raj Bhavan**
*   Rajasthan's **Raj Bhavan** has been renamed to **Lok Bhavan**.
*   This change was directed by the central government's Home Ministry, affecting eight states and one Union Territory.
*   The notification for Rajasthan's renaming was issued by Governor Haribhau Bagde.
*   The change is effective from **December 1, 2025**.
*   **Other related initiatives in Rajasthan:**
    *   The term "Mahamahim" (His Excellency) for the Governor has been replaced with "Rajyapal" (Governor), a change initiated by Kalraj Mishra.
    *   The tradition of the Governor sitting on a high chair has been stopped.
    *   The "Guard of Honor" tradition for the Governor has been discontinued.
