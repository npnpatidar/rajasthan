*   **Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) and Solar Plant Installation**
    *   A 250 kW solar rooftop plant has been installed on the RPSC building by the Rajasthan government.
    *   The plant is projected to produce 30,000 to 35,000 units of electricity per month, aiming to make the building energy self-sufficient.
    *   **About RPSC:**
        *   Established on December 22, 1949, based on the Rajasthan Lok Seva Aayog Ordinance 1949 (issued August 20, 1949).
        *   At the time of Rajasthan's formation, Public Service Commissions existed in Jaipur, Jodhpur, and Bikaner.
        *   Initially comprised 1 Chairman and 2 members. First Chairman: S.K. Ghosh.
        *   It is a constitutional body, mentioned in Part 14, Articles 315-323 of the Indian Constitution.
        *   Members are appointed by the Governor, can be suspended by the Governor, and removed by the President.
        *   Current composition: 1 Chairman and 10 members.
        *   Current Chairman: Utkal Ranjan Sahoo.
        *   Recently, two members, Manju Sharma and Sangeeta Arya, resigned.

*   **Rajasthan Police Academy**
    *   Recognized as the best training institute in the country for training police personnel.
    *   **History of Police Training in Rajasthan:**
        *   First Police Training Center: Established in Chittorgarh Fort in 1949.
        *   Shifted to Kishangarh, Ajmer in 1950. The first batch of Rajasthan Police began training here on May 1, 1950.
        *   Shifted to Jaipur in 1975 and renamed Rajasthan Police Academy.
        *   Foundation stone laid in October 1975.
        *   Inaugurated by Bhairon Singh Shekhawat in July 1978.
