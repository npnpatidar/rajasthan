**Rajasthan Current Affairs (December 18, 2025)**

**Saral Raj Sakhi National Fair 2025**
*   **Event:** Saral Raj Sakhi National Fair 2025.
*   **Location:** Jawahar Kala Kendra, Jaipur.
*   **Dates:** December 18, 2025 – January 4, 2026.
*   **Inauguration:** Chief Minister Bhajan Lal Sharma.
*   **Organizer:** Rajeevika (Rajasthan Rural Livelihood Development Council).
    *   Established: October 2011.
    *   Brand Ambassador: Ruma Devi (Barmer).
*   **Related Rajeevika Initiatives:**
    *   **Mission Samriddh Didi:** A month-long program offering loans to women.
    *   **Bartan Bank Yojana (Utensil Bank Scheme):**
        *   Announced: Budget 2025-26 (Green Budget).
        *   First phase: 1000 Gram Panchayats selected.
        *   Funding: ₹1 lakh per Gram Panchayat to purchase 400-piece utensil sets to reduce plastic use.
        *   Rental: ₹3 per utensil set for general families; 50% discount for SC/ST/BPL.
        *   First launched in Khairabad, Kota.
    *   **Mahila Nidhi:** Started August 10, 2022; provides loans up to ₹40,000 within 48 hours.

**GPS-Equipped Drone Pilot Project**
*   **Project:** Pilot project for transporting samples and organs using GPS-equipped drones.
*   **Location:** Jaipur.
*   **Initiator:** Mahatma Gandhi Medical College, Jaipur.
*   **Purpose:** To streamline organ/sample transport and eliminate the need for green corridors.
*   **Context:**
    *   SMS Hospital (Jaipur) has seen the launch of the state's first air ambulance, plasma bank, heart transplant, robotic lab, and sports injury center. It was also honored for organ donation.
    *   Mahatma Gandhi Medical College (Jaipur) received Central Government approval to develop a cancer vaccine.
    *   AIIMS previously initiated medical services using drones in Abu Road, Sirohi.

**Cyber Fusion Centers**
*   **Initiative:** Central Government establishing 20 Cyber Fusion Centers across India to combat cybercrime.
*   **Rajasthan's Contribution:** Five police districts in Rajasthan have been selected for these centers.
*   **Selected Police Districts:** Bhiwadi, Alwar, Khairthal-Tijara, Deeg, and Sawai Madhopur.
*   **Rajasthan Police Action:** "Operation Cyber Shield" launched by Rajasthan Police to control cybercrime.
