## Rajasthan Current Affairs

*   **State-Level Exhibition:**
    *   Inaugurated on December 15, 2025, by Bhajan Lal Sharma.
    *   Marked the completion of the Rajasthan government's two-year tenure.
    *   Exhibition programs were held from December 11 to 25.
    *   Specific events included:
        *   December 12: Rajasthan Digi Fest Hackathon.
        *   December 13: Road Safety Program (from Amar Jawan Jyoti).
        *   December 14: Cleanliness Program.
        *   December 15: Main program featuring the state-level exhibition at Jawahar Kala Kendra (established 1993, designed by Charles Correa).
    *   Theme of the second anniversary: "Nav Utthan, Nayi Pehchan, Badhta Rajasthan, Hamara Rajasthan."

*   **Voices of Harvest Award 2025:**
    *   Awarded in New Delhi at the Pepsico India Voices of Harvest Conference and Awards.
    *   **Gayatri Devi (Ganganagar):** Honored for Agri-Entrepreneurship (innovation in agriculture).
    *   **Om Prakash Sharma:** Honored for Water Conservation.
    *   **Jitendra Singh Ranawat (Chittorgarh):**
        *   Cultivated litchi for the first time in Rajasthan.
        *   His work was discussed by PM Narendra Modi in the 121st episode of "Mann Ki Baat."

*   **Railway Corridor Approval:**
    *   Union Ministry of Railways approved a new rail corridor from Jodhpur to Pachpadra.
    *   **Reason:** To support the development of the oil refinery at Pachpadra.
    *   **Pachpadra Oil Refinery Details:**
        *   **Inauguration:** January 16, 2018, by Narendra Modi.
        *   **Joint Venture:** HPCL (74%) and Rajasthan Government (26%).
        *   **Capacity:** 9 Million Metric Tons Per Annum (MMTPA).
        *   **Budget:** ₹72,937 Crore.
        *   **Debt-Equity Ratio:** 2:1 (HPCL:Rajasthan Government).
        *   **Standard:** BS-VI compliant.
        *   **Associated Centre:** Refinery Skill Dedicated Centre in Jodhpur.

*   **Mission Samriddh Didi Abhiyan:**
    *   Launched by **Rajeevika** (Rajasthan Grameen Aajeevika Vikas Parishad).
    *   **Rajeevika Background:**
        *   Registered in October 2011.
        *   **Standing Chairman:** Chief Minister.
        *   **Executive Chairman:** Chief Secretary (currently V. Srinivas).
        *   **Brand Ambassador:** Ruma Devi.
    *   **Previous Initiative:** "Mahila Nidhi" (established August 10, 2022, with support from Telangana's Stree Nidhi).
        *   Aimed to provide financial assistance for self-employment to women/groups.
        *   Loans up to ₹400,000 disbursed within 48 hours.
        *   Interest rate: 2.5% (to be reduced to 1.5% as per Budget 2025-26).
    *   **Mission Samriddh Didi Abhiyan (New Initiative):**
        *   One-month special program launched from December 12, 2025.
        *   Aims to provide financial loans to women/women self-help groups to make them economically prosperous.
    *   **Other "Didi" Programs:**
        *   **Namo Didi Drone Training Program:** To train women in drone operation for agricultural development.
        *   **Lakhpati Didi Program:** Aims to train 20 lakh women in Rajasthan to earn ₹1 lakh annually through skill development.
        *   **Solar Didi Program:** Aims to train 25,000 women (as per Budget 2025-26) to promote green energy.

*   **Flying Schools in Rajasthan:**
    *   Rajasthan government will operate eight flying schools.
    *   **Existing:** Kishanagarh, Ajmer.
    *   **Budget 2024-25:** Proposed three flying institutes in Kishanagarh (Ajmer), Hamirgarh (Bhilwara), and Jhalawar.
    *   **Budget 2025-26:** Proposed Flying Training Organizations in Pratapgarh, Jhalawar, and Jhunjhunu.

*   **Philippines Soft Sugarcane Cultivation:**
    *   **Antim Kumar Jain:** A farmer from Chhoti Sadri, Pratapgarh, successfully cultivated a soft variety of sugarcane from the Philippines.
    *   This sugarcane is sweeter, has more juice, and is easier to consume.
    *   **Pratapgarh's "Panch Gaurav" (Five Glories):**
        *   **Agriculture:** Kalonji
        *   **Forest Produce:** Tendu leaves (used for bidis)
        *   **Industry:** Thewa Art
        *   **Tourism:** Sita Mata Wildlife Sanctuary
        *   **Sports:** Athletics

*   **Youth Para Asian Games 2025 (Dubai):**
    *   Rajasthan athletes won six medals: two gold, two silver, and two bronze.

## Rajasthan G.K. Dose (558):

*   **Geography:**
    *   Lines showing East-West and North-South width intersect at Nagaur (specifically, Lampolai).
    *   North-South length: 826 km.
    *   East-West length: 869 km.
    *   Total land boundary: 5920 km (1070 km international, 4850 km domestic).
*   **History:**
    *   **Ahar Culture:** Known for Red-Black Pottery culture.
    *   **Ancient India's Tata Nagar:** Red (Tonk).
    *   **Nath Prashasti:** Related to Mewar kingdom, found in Eklingji Temple.
    *   **Forts:**
        *   Sir Thomas Roe met Mughal Emperor Jahangir at Magazine Fort, Ajmer.
        *   Rawal Jaisal Bhati constructed Jaisalmer Fort (Sonar Fort).
        *   "Ek Khamba Mahal" (One-Pillar Palace/Watchtower) built by Ajit Singh.
*   **Political Science:**
    *   **Shortest-serving Chief Secretary of Rajasthan:** Rajeev Swaroop.
    *   **District Collector as Chairman of District Disaster Management Authority:** Under Disaster Management Act, 2005.

## Previous Year Questions:

*   **Rajasthan's share of India's total land area:** 10.4% (approx).
*   **Difference in North-South and East-West expansion:** 43 km (869 - 826 = 43).
*   **Rajasthan's Desert Name:** Thar Desert.
*   **Direction of Desert Expansion in Rajasthan:** West to East.
*   **Steep, Hilly Terrain in Sirohi District:** Bhakar.
*   **Origin of Winter Rainfall (Western Disturbances):** Mediterranean Sea and Caspian Sea.
*   **Months for Western Disturbance Rainfall:** December and January.
*   **Predominant Soil Type in Jaipur, Dausa, Alwar:** Alfisols (due to being a plain area).
*   **Predominant Soil Type in Banas River Basin:** Mixed Red and Yellow (or Brown soil).
*   **Rajasthan's Decadal Population Growth Rate (2011 Census):** 21.3%.
