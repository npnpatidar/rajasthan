### Census 2011

*   **First Census:** 1872, under Viceroy Lord Mayo.
*   **Decadal Census Start:** 1881, under Viceroy Lord Ripon, with a 10-year interval.
*   **First Census Commissioner of India:** W.C. Plowden.
*   **2011 Census:**
    *   15th census overall.
    *   7th census since India's independence (from 1951).
    *   14th decadal census.
    *   **Commissioner:** C. Chandramouli.
    *   **Motto:** "Our Census, Our Future."
    *   **India's Population:** 121.09 crore (second globally at the time, now first).
    *   **Decadal Growth (2001-2011):** 17.7%.
    *   **Gender Distribution:**
        *   Males: 51.47%
        *   Females: 48.53%
*   **"Great Divide" Year:** 1921. During the 1911-1921 decade, population decreased due to famine and epidemics.
