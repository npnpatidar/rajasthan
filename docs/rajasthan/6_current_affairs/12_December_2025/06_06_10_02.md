### International Relations & Agreements

*   **India-Russia Relations:**
    *   Russian President Vladimir Putin visited India on December 4-5 for the 23rd India-Russia Annual Meeting.
    *   **RELOS Agreement:** India and Russia signed the Reciprocal Exchange of Logistics Support (RELOS) agreement.
    *   RELOS is a bilateral military logistic pact for refuelling, supply, repair, and maintenance of armed forces.
    *   A similar logistics agreement, LEMOA (Logistics Exchange Memorandum of Agreement), exists between India and the USA.

*   **Joint Military Training Exercises:**
    *   **Harimau Shakti:** Joint military training exercise between India and Malaysia, held from December 5-18 in Mahajan Field, Bikaner, Rajasthan. India's Dogra Regiment led the exercise.
    *   Other India-Malaysia exercises: Samudra Lakshman, Udara Shakti.
    *   **Garud Shakti:** 10th edition of this joint exercise between India and Indonesia, held in Bakloh, Himachal Pradesh.
    *   Another India-Indonesia exercise: Samudra Shakti.

### Environment & Climate Action

*   **Ozone Hole over Antarctica:** A record shrinkage has been observed in the ozone hole over Antarctica.
    *   The ozone layer (in the stratosphere) protects against harmful UV radiation, preventing skin cancer.
    *   Ozone concentration is measured in Dobson units.
    *   The ozone hole was first discovered by Fourman (England) in 1985.
    *   **Montreal Protocol (1987, Montreal, Canada):** Established to ban ozone-depleting substances like CFCs (chlorofluorocarbons/Freon), used as coolants.
    *   **Kigali Agreement (2016, Kigali, Rwanda):** Focuses on phasing down HFCs (hydrofluorocarbons), which replaced CFCs but are potent greenhouse gases.
