**MGNREGA Scheme Updates**
*   **New Name:** "Pujya Bapu Gramin Rojgar Yojana" (effective December 12).
*   **Background:**
    *   **NREGA Act:** 2005.
    *   **NREGA Launch:** February 2, 2006 (100 days employment, started in 200 districts).
    *   **Renamed to MGNREGA:** October 2, 2009.
*   **Key Provisions (current):**
    *   **Employment Days in Rajasthan:** 125 days (100 days + 25 additional days on demand).
    *   **Additional Days for Specific Tribes:** 50 additional days (total 150) for Sahariya and Kathodi tribes, and specially-abled individuals.
    *   **Minimum Wage:** â‚¹240.
    *   **Employment Guarantee:** Employment must be provided within 15 days of application.
    *   **Unemployment Allowance:** If employment is not provided within 15 days, 25% of the wage is given as an allowance for the first 30 days.
    *   **Work Location:** Within a 5 km radius. If work is provided beyond 5 km, an additional 10% of the wage is paid.
    *   **Women Participation:** 1/3 of beneficiaries must be women.

**Green Hydrogen and Zinc Battery Technology**
*   **Developer:** IIT Jodhpur.
*   **Innovations:**
    *   Technology to produce cheap green hydrogen directly from seawater.
    *   Development of super-safe zinc batteries (as an alternative to expensive lithium batteries).
*   **Green Hydrogen Energy Context in Rajasthan:**
    *   **Rajasthan Green Hydrogen Energy Policy:** September 29, 2023.
    *   **Policy Target:** 2000 KTPA (Kilotonnes Per Annum) production by 2030.
    *   **Hydro Energy Plants in Rajasthan:**
        *   First unit: Shahbad, Baran.
        *   Second unit: Brahmani River, Chittorgarh.
    *   **North India's Hydro Energy Hub:** IIT Jodhpur.
    *   **Lithium Reserves:** Found at Degana, Nagaur.
