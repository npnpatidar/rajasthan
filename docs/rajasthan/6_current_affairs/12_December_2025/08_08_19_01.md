**Rajasthan Current Affairs - December 8, 2025**

**Aravalli Rocks and Origin of Life**
*   Mohanlal Sukhadia University, Udaipur, discovered chemical clues in Aravalli rocks in the Iswal area of Udaipur to understand the origin of life on Earth.
*   The Aravalli range is the world's oldest mountain range, formed in the Pre-Cambrian era.
*   It acts as a barrier to the eastern expansion of the Western Desert and is a relict folded mountain range.
*   Earlier, evidence of the origin of life and atmosphere was found from Chitter stones in Jodhpur.

**PM Surya Ghar Yojana and City Accelerator Program**
*   Rajasthan cities included in the City Accelerator Program to promote rooftop solar installations under the PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana are Jaipur, Jodhpur, Udaipur, and Ajmer.
*   **PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana:**
    *   Central government scheme launched on February 13, 2024.
    *   Aim: Install solar panels on 1 crore homes nationwide and provide 300 units of free electricity.
    *   Rajasthan's target: 5 lakh homes.
    *   Provides a subsidy of ₹78,000 for up to 3 kW solar panels.
*   **City Accelerator Program:**
    *   Aimed at further promoting the PM Surya Ghar scheme.
    *   100 cities selected nationwide, including the four mentioned from Rajasthan.

**High-Tech Farming Model Village**
*   Kundalpur village in Sikar is a model village practicing high-tech vegetable farming using greenhouse and shednet technology, inspired by Israel.
*   Over 40 greenhouses and shednet facilities are installed, involving over 1500 people in farming.
*   The village generates an annual agricultural produce worth ₹50 crore.
*   **Other notable villages:**
    *   Chaup village: Location of the upcoming Anil Agarwal Cricket Stadium.
    *   Menar village: Included as a Ramsar Site (wetland).
    *   Piplantri village: Known for planting 111 trees upon the birth of a daughter; a recent documentary was made about the village.
