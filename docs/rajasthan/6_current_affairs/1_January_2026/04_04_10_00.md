### Global Geopolitical Shifts: BRICS and Expansion

*   **Original Membership:** Brazil, Russia, India, China, South Africa.
*   **Recent Expansion (Effective January 1, 2024):** Five new permanent members were inducted.
*   **New Members:**
    *   Egypt
    *   Ethiopia
    *   Iran
    *   Saudi Arabia
    *   United Arab Emirates (UAE)
*   **Impact:** The expansion significantly increases the bloc's global share of oil production and expands its representation across the Middle East and Africa.
*   **Decision Context:** The 15th BRICS Summit (held in Johannesburg, South Africa) finalized the consensus on the new membership criteria.

### National Economic Initiative: PM Viskarma Scheme

This scheme focuses on supporting traditional artisans and craftspeople (Vishwakarmas) through financial and skill development aid.

*   **Launch Date:** September 17, 2023.
*   **Nodal Ministry:** Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME).
*   **Target Group:** 18 identified traditional trades (e.g., blacksmiths, potters, barbers, sculptors).
*   **Financial Allocation:** ₹13,000 crore budgeted over a five-year period (2023-2024 to 2027-2028).

| Component | Detail |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Skill Upgradation** | Basic and Advanced training (stipend of ₹500/day during training). |
| **Tool Kit Incentive** | Financial assistance of ₹15,000 for modern tool purchases. |
| **Credit Support (Collateral-Free)** | **Tranche 1:** Up to ₹1 lakh (repayable in 18 months).<br>**Tranche 2:** Up to ₹2 lakh (repayable in 30 months). |
| **Interest Rate** | Concessional interest rate of 5%. |
| **Digital Transactions** | Provision of ₹1 per successful digital transaction (up to 100 transactions/month). |

### Indian Space Program: Chandrayaan-3 Mission Success

*   **Objective:** Achieve a safe and soft landing on the lunar surface, demonstrate rover mobility, and conduct in-situ scientific experiments.
*   **Landing Date:** August 23, 2023.
*   **Landing Site:** Near the lunar South Pole region (designated as Shiv Shakti Point).
*   **Landing Module Name:** *Vikram*.
*   **Rover Name:** *Pragyan*.
*   **Key Scientific Findings:**
    *   Detected minor lunar seismic activity near the landing site.
    *   Confirmed the presence of Aluminum (Al), Calcium (Ca), Iron (Fe), Chromium (Cr), Titanium (Ti), Manganese (Mn), Silicon (Si), and Oxygen (O) near the South Pole using the Laser-Induced Breakdown Spectroscope (LIBS).
    *   Recorded temperature variation of the lunar surface topsoil using the ChaSTE payload (Chandra’s Surface Thermo-physical Experiment).
*   **Distinction:** India became the fourth nation globally to achieve a soft lunar landing and the first to land near the South Pole.
