### International Space Policy: Artemis Accords

*   **Article 9 Revision:** New amendment requires signatory nations to submit annual resource utilization reports for lunar operations.
    *   **Purpose:** Standardize protocols concerning In-Situ Resource Utilization (ISRU).
    *   **Deadline Change:** Moved the submission window from Q4 to Q2, effective starting 2025.
*   **Signatory Status:** The current total of nations stands at 42, an increase of 7 signatories since the beginning of the year (previously 35).
