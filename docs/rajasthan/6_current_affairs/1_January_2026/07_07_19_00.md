## International Geopolitics and Organization Expansion

### BRICS Membership Changes (January 2024)

*   **Expanded Membership:** The BRICS bloc officially inducted five new permanent members, increasing the total count from five to ten nations.
*   **New Members Added:**
    *   Saudi Arabia
    *   United Arab Emirates (UAE)
    *   Iran
    *   Egypt
    *   Ethiopia
*   **Significance of Expansion:**
    *   The expansion dramatically increases the collective global GDP share and population representation of the bloc.
    *   Enhances focus on de-dollarization efforts, particularly through increased trade settlement in local currencies among member states.
    *   Iran’s inclusion strengthens the bloc’s strategic position in energy supply routes.
