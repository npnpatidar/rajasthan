*   **Inflation Targets:** Most major central banks maintained existing 2% core inflation targets, prioritizing price stability over short-term growth stimulus.
*   **Interest Rate Action:** The Federal Reserve held the benchmark rate steady for the third consecutive quarter, citing "persistent, albeit slowing, core services inflation."
*   **Fiscal Stimulus Comparison (Q2 Projections):**

*   **Commodity Markets:** Oil futures saw a 4% increase due to unexpected supply cuts announced by the OPEC+ coalition; supply concerns remain focused on the Strait of Hormuz.

### Geopolitical Updates: South Asia and Cybersecurity

*   **Regional Cooperation:** The recent SAARC ministerial meeting concluded with agreements on cross-border digital payments infrastructure but failed to reach consensus on a unified energy grid strategy.
*   **Bilateral Ties (Country X and Y):** Negotiations regarding the disputed maritime border have stalled following unilateral deep-sea drilling permits issued by Country Y.
    *   Focus remains on de-escalation via UN-backed maritime law tribunals.
*   **Cyber Warfare Doctrine:** Several nations updated their cybersecurity policy, moving from passive defense to "active deterrence." This doctrine permits preemptive cyber engagement against verifiable state-sponsored threats originating from foreign servers.

*   **National Green Hydrogen Mission:** The government allocated $2.5 billion for pilot projects across three industrial clusters over the next two years.
    *   **Primary Goals:** Achieve 5 million metric tons per annum (MMTPA) of Green Hydrogen production capacity by 2030.
*   **Data Governance Bill:** Key provisions finalized include mandatory data localization for sensitive personal data and the establishment of an independent Data Protection Board for compliance oversight and penalty imposition.
