## Global Minimum Corporate Tax (GMCT)

*   **Objective:** To ensure Multinational Enterprises (MNEs) pay a minimum tax rate globally, irrespective of their operating location.
*   **Minimum Rate:** 15%.
*   **Framework Name:** Pillar Two initiative (spearheaded by the OECD).
*   **Target Threshold:** Applies to MNEs with global revenues exceeding 750 million euros.
*   **Rationale:** Designed to limit "race to the bottom" tax competition between countries.

## Blue Flag Certification

*   **Definition:** A voluntary eco-label award given to sustainable boating tourism operators, marinas, and beaches.
*   **Governing Organization:** Foundation for Environmental Education (FEE), headquartered in Denmark.
*   **Criteria:** Certification requires meeting 33 stringent standards across four main categories:
    1.  Environmental Education and Information
    2.  Water Quality
    3.  Environmental Management
    4.  Safety and Services
*   **Indian Implementation:**
    *   **Coordinating Ministry:** Ministry of Environment, Forest, and Climate Change (MoEFCC).
    *   **Implementing Body:** Society of Integrated Coastal Management (SICOM).
    *   **Current Status (Statistical Detail):** India currently has 12 Blue Flag certified beaches.
