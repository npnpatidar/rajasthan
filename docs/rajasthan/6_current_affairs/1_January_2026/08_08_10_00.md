**Geopolitical Flashpoint: Red Sea Security**

*   **Conflict Actors:** Houthi rebels, allegedly supported by Iran, executing attacks against commercial maritime traffic.
*   **Target Zone:** Primarily the Bab el-Mandeb Strait, a critical narrow gateway connecting the Red Sea to the Gulf of Aden.
*   **Economic Impact:**
    *   Significant diversion of container shipping traffic away from the Suez Canal route.
    *   Increase in transit time by approximately 10–14 days due to vessels opting for the longer Cape of Good Hope route.
    *   Maritime insurance premiums for war risk coverage have sharply escalated.
*   **Response:** The multinational coalition "Operation Prosperity Guardian" (OPG), led by the US, was deployed to safeguard international shipping lanes in the region.

**Central Bank Monetary Policy Review**

*   **Repo Rate:** Maintained unchanged at 6.5% during the recent review, marking the sixth consecutive pause.
*   **Inflation Outlook:** Projections suggest consumer price index (CPI) inflation will average 5.0% for the current fiscal year.
*   **Growth Forecast:** Gross Domestic Product (GDP) growth forecast upgraded to 7.0% for the next fiscal year, citing strong domestic demand and investment uptick.
*   **Liquidity Management:** Focus shifted towards calibrated withdrawal of accommodation to manage excess liquidity in the banking system without tightening credit conditions abruptly.

**National Scientific Development: Gaganyaan Mission**

*   **Objective:** India’s first human spaceflight mission.
*   **Timeline:** Target launch window tentatively set for 2025.
*   **Key Components:**
    *   Development of the indigenous heavy-lift launch vehicle, Launch Vehicle Mark-3 (LVM3).
    *   Successful execution of Flight Test Vehicle Abort Mission-1 (TV-D1) to demonstrate crew escape capabilities.
*   **Training:** Astronaut trainees are undergoing specialized module training in designated centers focused on survival, recovery, and medical protocols.
