### International Economic Groupings: G20 New Delhi Declaration

*   **Global Biofuel Alliance (GBA):** Launched during the summit.
    *   Objective: Accelerate global adoption of biofuels by facilitating technical cooperation and standard setting.
    *   Founding Members include the United States, Brazil, and India.
*   **India-Middle East-Europe Economic Corridor (IMEC):** Proposed multinational rail and shipping network.
    *   Aims to enhance connectivity and boost economic integration between Asia, the Arabian Gulf, and Europe.
    *   Expected to significantly reduce transit times and costs compared to existing routes.
*   **Climate Finance Commitment:** Reaffirmed commitment to the $100 billion annual climate finance goal (developed countries to developing countries).
*   **Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI):** Emphasis placed on leveraging DPI for financial inclusion, particularly focusing on digital identity, real-time payment systems, and data exchange layers.
*   **Reform of Multilateral Development Banks (MDBs):** Agreed upon the need for MDBs to broaden their scope to address 21st-century challenges like climate change and pandemics, alongside poverty eradication.
