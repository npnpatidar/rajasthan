*   The Central Bank implemented a 25 basis point hike, the seventh consecutive increase since Q3 last year.
*   The bank projects inflation to remain above 4% for the next two quarters before stabilizing.
*   Key challenge cited: Wage growth exceeding productivity gains in the manufacturing sector.
*   Fiscal policy remains contractionary to align with monetary tightening efforts.

**International Relations: G7 Summit Key Outcomes (2024)**

*   **Ukraine Aid:** Agreement reached on utilizing frozen Russian sovereign assets to provide a $50 billion loan facility for Ukraine.
    *   Mechanism: Repayment guaranteed by future interest earnings from the assets.
*   **Climate & Energy:** Commitment to triple global renewable energy capacity by 2030.
    *   Focus on phasing out reliance on coal power, specifically naming accelerated timelines for three member states.
*   **China Trade:** Declaration emphasizing "de-risking, not decoupling" regarding trade relations.
    *   Shared concern over industrial overcapacity and non-market practices affecting global pricing.
*   **Global Health Initiative:** Pledges renewed to fully fund the Pandemic Preparedness and Response Initiative (PPRI) for the 2024-2025 cycle.

**Science and Technology: Artemis II Mission Preparation**

*   **Status:** The crew capsule, Orion, has successfully completed final integrated stress testing at the Kennedy Space Center.
*   **Launch Vehicle:** The Space Launch System (SLS) core stage is undergoing final structural checks ahead of stacking operations.
*   **Crew Responsibilities:**
    *   Mission duration estimated at approximately 10 days.
    *   Primary objective: Crewed test flight around the Moon to validate life support systems and communication protocols.
    *   Trajectory: Free-return circumlunar trajectory; no landing attempt scheduled.
*   **Involved Agencies:** NASA (primary), Canadian Space Agency (CSA) contributing critical robotic arms and crew member.
