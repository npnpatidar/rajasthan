**Global Geopolitics: BRICS 2024 Expansion**

*   **15th BRICS Summit (2023):** Held in Johannesburg, South Africa.
*   **Key Decision:** Agreement to expand the bloc for the first time since 2010.
*   **Goal of Expansion:** To enhance global south representation and balance the influence of Western-dominated institutions.
*   **Implementation Date:** New members officially joined on January 1, 2024.
*   **New Membership Structure:**

**National Energy Policy: Green Hydrogen Mission**

*   **Mission Name:** National Green Hydrogen Mission (NGHM).
*   **Objective:** To position the nation as a global hub for the production, utilization, and export of Green Hydrogen and its derivatives.
*   **Financial Commitment:** Initial outlay approved is [Specific Figure] crore rupees.
*   **Targeted Outcomes (by 2030):**
    *   Development of 5 million metric tonnes (MMT) annual Green Hydrogen production capacity.
    *   Over ₹8 lakh crore in total investments.
    *   Reduction in fossil fuel imports by over ₹1 lakh crore.

    *   Pilot Projects in steel, transport, and shipping sectors.

**Space Exploration and Scientific Findings**

*   **Mission:** Chandrayaan-3 (India's third lunar mission).
*   **Landing Site:** Near the south polar region of the Moon (first mission to achieve this).
*   **Key Instrument Findings (Pragyan Rover/Vikram Lander):**
    *   **Temperature Measurement:** ChaSTE payload recorded significant temperature variations between the lunar surface and a depth of 10 cm, confirming poor thermal conductivity of the regolith.
    *   **Composition:** Confirmed the presence of Sulphur (S) using Laser-Induced Breakdown Spectroscopy (LIBS), along with Aluminium (Al), Calcium (Ca), Iron (Fe), and Titanium (Ti).
    *   **Seismic Activity:** The ILSA payload recorded a lunar seismic event, which is being investigated for natural or impact origin.
