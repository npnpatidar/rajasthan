## International Relations: Climate Policy Updates

*   **COP 28 Outcomes (Dubai):**
    *   **Key Agreement:** Transitioning away from fossil fuels, marking the first time such language was included in a final COP text.
    *   **Loss and Damage Fund:** Officially operationalized.
        *   **Initial Status:** Managed by the World Bank on an interim basis.
        *   **Initial Pledges:** Exceeded $700 million upon launch (including contributions from the EU, UAE, and the US).
    *   **Global Goal on Adaptation (GGA):** Framework established, focusing on setting clear targets for enhancing resilience to climate change impacts.

## National Economy and Monetary Policy

*   **Reserve Bank of India (RBI) Policy Review:**
    *   **Repo Rate:** Maintained unchanged at 6.5% for the sixth consecutive policy meeting.
    *   **Stance:** Focus remains on the "withdrawal of accommodation" to ensure inflation aligns with the target.

## Science and Technology Development

*   **Space Missions (NASA & ISRO):**
    *   **Artemis III (NASA):** Mission date delayed from late 2025 to September 2026, citing necessary safety checks and hardware development delays for the Starship Human Landing System.
    *   **Gaganyaan (ISRO):** Successfully completed the final series of tests on the Crew Module Escape System (CMES), validating crew safety protocols during launch abort scenarios.

*   **Defense Technology:**
    *   **Agni-V Ballistic Missile:** Successfully flight-tested with Multiple Independently Targetable Reentry Vehicle (MIRV) technology.
        *   **Significance:** Allows a single missile to deliver multiple warheads to different targets simultaneously, significantly enhancing deterrence capability.
