## Global Geopolitics and Emerging Alliances

*   **BRICS Expansion (Effective Jan 1, 2024):** The economic bloc expanded its membership from five founding nations (Brazil, Russia, India, China, South Africa) to ten.
    *   **New Members Added:** Egypt, Ethiopia, Iran, Saudi Arabia, and the United Arab Emirates (UAE).
    *   **Objective:** To increase the bloc's global economic influence and strengthen the representation of the Global South in international governance.

*   **Red Sea Maritime Crisis:** Ongoing security threats targeting commercial shipping in the Bab-el-Mandeb Strait and the Gulf of Aden.
    *   **Threat Actor:** Houthi militia groups operating from Yemen.
    *   **International Response:** Formation of the US-led **Operation Prosperity Guardian**, a multinational initiative established to ensure the freedom of navigation and protect vital international trade routes.

## International Trade and Economic Policy

*   **WTO 13th Ministerial Conference (MC13) – Abu Dhabi:**
    *   **Key Focus:** Reform of the World Trade Organization’s Dispute Settlement Mechanism (DSM).
    *   **Electronic Transmissions Moratorium:** Members agreed to extend the temporary ban on imposing customs duties on electronic transmissions until the next Ministerial Conference (MC14) in 2026.

*   **Global Inflation Trends (Q1 2024):**
    *   While energy and major commodity prices have stabilized, core inflation remains persistently high across many OECD economies.
    *   **Primary Driver:** Sustained high labor costs and demand within the service sector.

## Science and Environmental Policy

| Mission/Event | Date/Location | Key Outcome |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Chandrayaan-3** | Aug 23, 2023 (Moon’s South Pole) | India became the first country to successfully soft-land near the lunar South Pole. Confirmed presence of Sulfur, Aluminum, and Iron on the surface. |
| **COP28 Climate Summit** | Dubai | Concluded the first **Global Stocktake** assessing progress toward the Paris Agreement goals. |

*   **COP28 Major Decision:** Attendees agreed to a global commitment to **"transition away"** from fossil fuels in energy systems, signaling a consensus on the need for sustained and rapid reductions in emissions.
*   **Target Goal:** Tripling global renewable energy capacity by 2030 to meet climate mitigation objectives.
