## India's National Space Policy (NSP) 2023

*   **Objective:** To govern the activities of the Indian space sector and promote participation from Non-Government Entities (NGEs).
*   **Key Roles Defined:**
    | Entity | Primary Function |
    | :--- | :--- |
    | **ISRO** | Focus on R&D, development of new technologies, and strategic national missions. |
    | **IN-SPACe** | Single-window regulatory and promotional body for NGEs. Authorizes and supervises space activities. |
    | **NewSpace India Ltd (NSIL)** | Commercial arm of ISRO, responsible for transfer of technologies and commercial exploitation of R&D outputs. |
*   NGEs are permitted to carry out activities in areas including satellite communication, data collection, and launch vehicle operation, subject to authorization.
*   The policy encourages academic institutions and private industry involvement in future space exploration missions.

## Geopolitical Crisis in Sudan (April 2023)

*   **Conflict Participants:** Fighting primarily between the Sudanese Armed Forces (SAF) and the Rapid Support Forces (RSF).
*   **Cause of Conflict:** A power struggle stemming from the failure to integrate the SAF and RSF under a unified military command, following the 2021 military coup.
*   **Impact:** Severe humanitarian crisis, focused heavily in the capital city, Khartoum. Widespread shortages of food, water, and medical supplies.
*   **International Response (Evacuation Missions):**
    *   **India:** Launched **Operation Kaveri** to evacuate Indian citizens trapped in the conflict zone.
    *   **Other Nations:** Multiple countries (including the US, UK, and France) conducted rapid evacuation efforts for their diplomatic staff and citizens.

## World Bank's Logistics Performance Index (LPI) 2023

*   The LPI measures the speed and reliability of supply chain performance across countries.
*   **Metrics Used:** The index is based on six key components: Customs, Infrastructure, International Shipments, Logistics Competence, Tracking & Tracing, and Timeliness.
*   **India's Performance:**
    *   India registered a notable jump in ranking from the previous index.
    *   **2023 Rank:** 38th (out of 139 countries).
    *   **Improvement Areas:** Significant progress recorded in the quality of infrastructure and the competence of logistics services.
*   The 2023 report emphasizes the increasing importance of digital infrastructure in improving trade logistics resilience.
