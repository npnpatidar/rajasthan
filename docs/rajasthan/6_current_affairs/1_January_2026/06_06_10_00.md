## Global Geopolitics: Red Sea Crisis

*   **Primary Actors:** Houthi rebels (Yemen).
*   **Target:** Commercial vessels navigating the Red Sea.
*   **Intent:** Actions framed as solidarity with Palestine following intensified conflict.
*   **Timeline:** Escalation began significantly post-October 2023.
*   **Impact on Trade:** Severe disruption of the Bab el-Mandeb Strait, impacting approximately 15% of global maritime trade flows.
*   **Multinational Response:** Establishment of "Operation Prosperity Guardian," a U.S.-led multinational task force focused on safeguarding international shipping routes.

## Domestic Policy: Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI) in India

| Component | Function/Achievement | Key Metric |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Aadhaar** | Digital Identity System | High national penetration rate. |
| **UPI** (Unified Payments Interface) | Instant, interoperable payment system. | Records billions of monthly transactions. |
| **DigiLocker** | Secure platform for issuance and verification of digital documents. | Used across various government departments. |

*   **New Initiative: Open Network for Digital Commerce (ONDC):**
    *   **Goal:** To democratize and decentralize India's e-commerce market.
    *   **Mechanism:** Enables seamless interaction between buyers, sellers, and logistics providers without relying on single dominant platform intermediaries.

## Global Climate Summit Updates

*   **COP29 Location:** Baku, Azerbaijan.
*   **Key Outcomes of COP28 (Dubai):**
    *   **Fossil Fuels:** Parties agreed, for the first time, to "transition away" from fossil fuels, emphasizing the need for accelerated action this decade.
    *   **Finance:** Operationalization of the Loss and Damage Fund to provide financial assistance to climate-vulnerable nations suffering immediate climate impacts.
