## International Summit Analysis: G7 2024

*   **Location:** Puglia, Italy.
*   **Duration:** June 13–15, 2024.
*   **Key Focus Areas:**
    *   **Ukraine Support:** Agreement on using frozen Russian sovereign assets (approximately $50 billion) to provide long-term loans to Ukraine.
    *   **China Policy:** Discussions focused on addressing China’s non-market practices and industrial overcapacity, specifically concerning green energy technologies (EVs and solar panels).
    *   **PGI Initiative:** Reaffirmation of commitment to the Partnership for Global Infrastructure and Investment (PGI), positioning it as an alternative financing model to China’s Belt and Road Initiative (BRI).

## Space Exploration Updates

*   **Artemis Program:** NASA confirmed the target date for the Artemis II crewed lunar flyby mission is scheduled for September 2025.
    *   **Objective:** To test the full operational capabilities of the Orion spacecraft with a human crew prior to landing missions.
    *   **Launch Vehicle:** Space Launch System (SLS).
*   **China's Chang'e 6 Mission:** Successfully returned lunar samples from the far side of the Moon.
    *   **Significance:** First ever samples retrieved from the far side, providing unique geological data not available from Earth-facing landing sites.

## Domestic Digital Infrastructure Milestones

*   **Unified Payments Interface (UPI):** Processed 14.04 billion transactions in May 2024, representing a year-on-year volume growth of 49%.
    *   **Key Contributors:** Third-party application providers (TPAPs) dominating transaction volume share.
*   **5G Deployment:** Achieved installation of over 400,000 5G base transceiver stations (BTS) nationwide by Q2 2024.
    *   **Goal:** Focus shifts from basic coverage rollout to enhancing 5G enterprise use cases in manufacturing and healthcare sectors.
