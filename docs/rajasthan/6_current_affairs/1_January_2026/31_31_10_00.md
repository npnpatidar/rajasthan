**National Infrastructure Initiative: Green Energy Transition Scheme (GETS-2025)**

*   **Objective:** To accelerate the integration of renewable energy sources into the national grid and modernize transmission infrastructure.
*   **Key Pillars of GETS-2025:**
    1.  **Distributed Generation:** Mandating rooftop solar capacity for all new commercial infrastructure projects over 5,000 sq. meters.
    2.  **Grid Hardening:** Allocation of $5 billion for smart grid technology implementation in coastal regions highly susceptible to climate events.
    3.  **Storage Capacity:** Incentivizing the establishment of four large-scale Pumped Hydro Storage (PHS) facilities by 2029.
*   **Funding Mechanism:** A blend of central government grants (70%) and private Public-Private Partnership (PPP) investment (30%).

**Economic Policy Review: Monetary Policy Committee (MPC) Decisions**

*   **Policy Rate Status:** The MPC unanimously decided to maintain the existing Repo Rate at 6.5%.
*   **Rationale for Rate Hold:**
    *   **Inflation Target:** Although CPI inflation is trending downwards, core inflation remains sticky, necessitating a cautious approach.
    *   **Growth Projections:** Current GDP growth forecasts remain robust (projected 6.8% for the fiscal year), supporting the decision to prioritize inflation stability over stimulating further growth.
    *   **Liquidity Management:** The central bank will continue using Variable Rate Reverse Repo (VRRR) auctions to absorb excess systemic liquidity.
