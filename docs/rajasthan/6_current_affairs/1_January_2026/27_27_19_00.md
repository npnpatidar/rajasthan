**Global Economic Policy Adjustments**

*   **Central Bank Rate Action:** The policy interest rate was raised by 25 basis points (0.25%).
    *   *Objective:* To manage persistent core inflation, currently tracked at 4.5%.
    *   *Expected Effect:* Cooling of demand in the housing sector and a marginal increase in variable-rate mortgage costs.
*   **Fiscal Stimulus Review:** Government announced a phased withdrawal of pandemic-era subsidies beginning Q4.
    *   Focus remains on infrastructure investment, specifically targeting digital connectivity projects ($50 billion allocation).
*   **Trade Negotiation Status:** Talks between the regional economic bloc and the Pacific nations have reached a preliminary agreement on reducing tariffs for agricultural goods by 15% over three years.

**International Relations and Climate Governance**

*   **Global Climate Summit (GCS) Outcomes:** Held in Geneva to establish updated emission targets.
    *   **Renewable Energy Pledge:** Participating nations committed to tripling global renewable energy capacity by 2030.
    *   **Funding Mechanism:** A new $500 billion fund established to assist developing nations in modernizing electrical grids and transitioning energy sources.

**Scientific and Technological Milestones**

*   **Aerospace Launch:** The *Sentinel-VI* weather satellite was successfully deployed into a Geostationary Earth Orbit (GEO).
    *   *Primary Function:* Real-time monitoring of tropical cyclone formation and oceanic currents.
    *   *Duration:* Expected operational lifespan of 15 years.
*   **Medical Research Breakthrough:** Researchers identified a novel antibody treatment effective against the highly resistant strain of Malaria (P. falciparum). Clinical trials are scheduled to enter Phase 2 next quarter.
*   **Quantum Computing:** A team demonstrated the stable entanglement of 12 fully functional qubits, setting a new benchmark for computational power density.
