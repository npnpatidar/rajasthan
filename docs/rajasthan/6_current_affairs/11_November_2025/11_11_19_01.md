## Mining Sector in Rajasthan

*   Rajasthan is the first state to conduct a pre-embedded auction of eight major mineral blocks in the country.
*   **Khan Evam Khanij Adhiniyam 1957:** Categorizes minerals into Major (Central Government responsibility) and Minor (State Government responsibility, generally building materials).
*   **Rajasthan Economic Survey 2024-25:**
    *   81 types of minerals available in Rajasthan.
    *   58 types of minerals produced in Rajasthan (22 Major, 36 Minor).
*   **Rajasthan New Mineral Policy 2024:**
    *   Launched: December 4, 2024.
    *   Duration: Until March 31, 2029, or until a new policy is introduced.
    *   Target: Increase produced minerals from 58 to 70 types by 2047.
    *   Target: Allot 50 Major Mineral Blocks by 2029-30.
    *   **GDP Contribution (Mineral Sector in Rajasthan):**
        *   Current: 3.4%
        *   Target: 5% by 2029-30.
        *   Target: 8% by 2047.
    *   **Employment Target (Mineral Sector in Rajasthan):**
        *   50 lakh by 2029-30.
        *   1 crore by 2047.
*   Rajasthan is the first state to implement the "pre-embedded" scheme (pre-preparing all NOCs before auction).
*   Eight Major Mineral blocks were allocated in Jodhpur for Limestone, which was recently classified as a Major Mineral. Rajasthan is the first state to allocate 8 Major Mineral blocks simultaneously.
