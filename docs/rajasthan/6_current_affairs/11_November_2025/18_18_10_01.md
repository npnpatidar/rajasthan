**Census 2011 Data**

*   **Overview:**
    *   Initial census began in 1872 (Viceroy Mayo).
    *   Scientific/Decennial census began in 1881 (Viceroy Ripon), setting a 10-year interval.
    *   First Census Commissioner of India: W.C. Plowden.
    *   2011 Census was the 15th overall, 7th since independence (1951), and 14th decennial.
    *   The 1911-1921 decade is known as the "Great Divide Year" (1921) due to population decrease caused by famine and epidemics.
    *   2011 Census Commissioner: C. Chandramouli.
    *   2011 Census Motto: "Our Census, Our Future."
    *   India's 2011 population: 121.09 crores, making it the second most populous nation (now first).
    *   Decennial growth rate (2001-2011): 17.7%.
    *   Gender distribution: Males 51.47%, Females 48.53%.
    *   Largest state by area: Rajasthan; Smallest: Goa.
    *   Largest Union Territory (UT) by area: Ladakh; Smallest: Lakshadweep.

*   **Population - State-wise:**
    *   **Most Populous States:**
        *   Uttar Pradesh (16.51% of India's population)
        *   Maharashtra
        *   Bihar
        *   West Bengal
    *   **Least Populous States:**
        *   Sikkim (0.05% of India's population)
        *   Mizoram
        *   Arunachal Pradesh
        *   Goa

*   **Decennial Growth Rate (2001-2011) - State-wise:**
    *   **Highest Growth:** Meghalaya (27.9%)
    *   **Lowest Growth:** Nagaland (-0.6%, only state with negative growth)

*   **Population Density - State/UT-wise:**
    *   India's average density: 382 persons/sq km.
    *   **Highest Density States:** Bihar (1106 persons/sq km)
    *   **Lowest Density States:** Arunachal Pradesh (17 persons/sq km)
    *   **Highest Density UT:** Delhi

*   **Sex Ratio (Females per 1000 Males) - State-wise:**
    *   India's average: 943.
    *   **Highest Sex Ratio States:** Kerala (1084, only state with >1000)
    *   **Lowest Sex Ratio States:** Haryana (879)
    *   **Child Sex Ratio (0-6 years):** Highest in Arunachal Pradesh (972), Lowest in Haryana (834).

*   **Literacy Rate - State-wise:**
    *   India's average: 73% (Males: 80.9%, Females: 64.6%).
    *   **Highest Literacy Rate States:** Kerala (94%)
    *   **Lowest Literacy Rate States:** Bihar (61.8%)

*   **Rural & Urban Population - State-wise:**
    *   **Highest Rural Population (percentage):** Himachal Pradesh (90%)
    *   **Highest Urban Population (percentage):** Goa (62.2%)
    *   **Highest Urban Population (numbers):** Maharashtra (5.08 crore)

*   **Scheduled Caste (SC) Population - State-wise:**
    *   **Highest SC Population (numbers):** Uttar Pradesh (~5.25 crore)
    *   **Highest SC Population (percentage):** Punjab (31.9%)

*   **Scheduled Tribe (ST) Population - State-wise:**
    *   **Highest ST Population (numbers):** Madhya Pradesh (~1.5 crore)
    *   **Highest ST Population (percentage):** Mizoram (94%)
