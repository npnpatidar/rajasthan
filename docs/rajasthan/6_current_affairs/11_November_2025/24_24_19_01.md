**Government Schemes and Projects**

*   **Doongri Dam Project:**
    *   Built under the Modified Parvati Kalisindh Chambal (P-K-C) Link Project.
    *   Beneficiary Districts: Karauli and Sawai Madhopur.
    *   Irrigation Area: Approximately 25,000 hectares (as per Economic Survey).
    *   Background: The P-K-C Link Project was formerly known as the Eastern Rajasthan Canal Project (ERCP) and later renamed Ram Jal Setu Link Project.
    *   Objective: Connect tributary rivers of Chambal.
    *   Partnership: Government of India (90%), Rajasthan (5%), Madhya Pradesh (5%).
    *   Total Beneficiaries (Ram Jal Setu Link Project): 17 districts and 4 lakh hectares.
    *   Phases:
        *   Phase 1: Navnera Dam construction.
        *   Phase 2: Doongri Dam (Sawai Madhopur) and Mej Barrage (Bundi) construction.
    *   Controversy: Government clarified only 16 villages would be affected, not 35-40, by the dam's submergence area.
*   **Mukhyamantri Ayushman Arogya Bal Sambal Yojana:**
    *   Launched: December 15, 2024, from Pinjar Pol Gaushala, Jaipur, during the Antyodaya Karyakram (part of the Rajasthan government's 1-year anniversary events).
    *   Objective: Provide financial assistance for children suffering from rare diseases.
    *   Beneficiaries: Rajasthan residents, aged 0-18 years, suffering from rare diseases.
    *   Financial Aid: Up to ₹50 lakh for treatment.
    *   Caregiver Allowance: ₹5,000 per month.
    *   Authorized Treatment Centers (currently): AIIMS Jodhpur and JK Lon Hospital Jaipur.
    *   Related government 1-year anniversary events:
        *   December 13, 2024, Kayad Ajmer: Kisan Sammelan, launch of Mangala Pashu Bima Yojana (initially 21 lakh animals, increased to 42 lakh).
        *   December 14, 2024, Udaipur: Mahila Sammelan, discussed milk distribution at Anganwadi centers.
