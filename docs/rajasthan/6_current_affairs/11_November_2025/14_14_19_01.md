**Kharif Crop Girdawari Report 2025-26**
*   The Rajasthan Agriculture Department released the Kharif Crop Girdawari Report for 2025-26.
*   Based on the report, 2,961 villages across six districts of Rajasthan have been declared 'famine-affected' (अभावग्रस्त) due to crop damage.
*   The six affected districts are Ajmer, Alwar, Jhunjhunu, Pali, Kota, and Banswara.

**First Incentive Calculator**
*   Rajasthan launched its first incentive calculator named "Labh Calc."
*   "Labh Calc" is designed to estimate the incentives and benefits available to new industries or existing units expanding under the Rajasthan Investment Promotion Scheme (RIPS) 2024.
*   RIPS 2024 was introduced on December 4, 2024, and will be effective until March 31, 2029. Its primary objective is to boost investment in Rajasthan.

**Rajasthan MSME Policy 2024**
*   The Rajasthan MSME Policy 2024 was implemented on December 4, 2024, and will be effective until March 31, 2029.
*   This new policy replaces the MSME Policy Package 2022.
*   It is designed as a "One Stop Shop" for all incentives provided by the state government for MSME units.
*   The Board of Investment, established under this policy, is chaired by the Chief Minister (CM), not the Industry Secretary, and is responsible for approving investment proposals.
*   Key measures of the policy include promoting entrepreneurship, cluster development, and market promotion.
*   MSME Day is observed on September 17.
*   Rajasthan's first Handicraft Policy was also introduced on September 17, 2022.
