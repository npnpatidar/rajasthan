### International Cooperative Alliance (ICA) Global Ranking

*   In the ICA global ranking based on per capita GDP:
    *   **Amul** (dairy company, Anand, Gujarat) ranked 1st worldwide.
    *   **IFFCO** (fertilizer company, Delhi) ranked 2nd worldwide.
*   The year 2025 is designated as the **International Year of Cooperatives**.
*   India established a **Ministry of Cooperation** in 2021, with Amit Shah as its first minister.
*   The 97th Constitutional Amendment in 2011 added Article 43B and Part 9B to the Indian Constitution, promoting cooperative societies.

### Project Cheetah

*   Cheetahs were declared extinct in India in 1952.
*   **Project Cheetah** was launched on September 17, 2022, to reintroduce cheetahs to India.
*   Previously, 8 cheetahs were brought from **Namibia** to Kuno National Park, Madhya Pradesh, in September 2022. Cheetahs were also brought from **South Africa**.
*   India will now bring **eight more cheetahs from Botswana** to Kuno National Park, Madhya Pradesh.
*   **Botswana** is known for the Kalahari Desert (home to the Bushmen tribe) and its capital is Gaborone.
*   Current cheetah habitats in India include Kuno National Park and Gandhi Sagar Sanctuary, both in Madhya Pradesh. A third site, **Naurodoi** (Madhya Pradesh), has been proposed.
*   The first cheetah born in India was named **Mukhi**, whose mother was Jwala.

### Indian Navy's Indigenous Ship

*   The Indian Navy will commission **INS Ikshak** in Kochi.
*   "Ikshak" means "Guide" or "Surveyor." It is a **survey vessel** designed for hydrographic surveys.
*   It was built by **Garden Reach Shipbuilders & Engineers (GRSE)** in Kolkata.
*   The commissioning will be overseen by Dinesh Tripathi, the current Chief of the Indian Navy, for the Indian Navy's Southern Command.
*   Previous indigenous survey vessels include INS Sandhayak (first) and INS Nirdeshak (second).

### World Tsunami Awareness Day

*   **World Tsunami Awareness Day** is observed annually on **November 5**.
*   The word "Tsunami" is Japanese, meaning "harbor wave," and is primarily caused by **underwater earthquakes**.
*   The theme for the day is "Ready for Tsunami."
*   **International Bio Reserves Day** is celebrated on **November 3**.
*   Currently, **13 of India's 18 biosphere reserves** are included in UNESCO's World Network of Biosphere Reserves. The latest addition is the **Cold Desert Biosphere Reserve in Himachal Pradesh**.
