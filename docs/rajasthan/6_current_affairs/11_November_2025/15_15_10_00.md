**WHO Global TB Report 2025**

*   India recorded the highest global decline in tuberculosis (TB) cases.
*   TB cases in India decreased by 21% from 237 per 100,000 people in 2015 to 187 per 100,000 in 2024.
*   The global average decline in TB cases was 12%.
*   TB-related deaths in India reduced from 28 per 100,000 in 2015 to 21 per 100,000 in 2025.
*   TB is caused by bacteria (Mycobacterium tuberculosis) and primarily affects the respiratory system.
*   Robert Koch discovered the TB bacteria. BCG is the TB vaccine.

**Defense & Security**

*   **"Garuda 2025" Air Force Exercise:**
    *   Joint exercise between India and France, to be hosted in France.
    *   India will participate with Rafale and Sukhoi aircraft.
    *   Other India-France exercises include Shakti, Varuna, and FRINJEX.
*   **"Operation Akhand Prahar" Exercise:**
    *   Conducted by the Indian Army in Jaisalmer, Rajasthan.
    *   Aimed at testing the capabilities of the Rudra Brigade.
*   **Mudnio Airbase:**
    *   India inaugurated the Mudnio Airbase in Ladakh.
    *   It is located near the Line of Actual Control (LAC), Pangong Lake, and Galwan Valley.
    *   This is Ladakh's fourth airbase, in addition to Leh, Kargil, and Thoise.
    *   Situated at an altitude of 13,710 feet.
    *   A C13J Super Hercules aircraft made the first landing at the airbase.
*   **Durga Drone Squadron:**
    *   The Border Security Force (BSF) launched the first all-women drone operation unit, named "Durga Drone Squadron."
    *   Training for the squadron took place in Gwalior.
    *   The BSF, established in 1965, is deployed along the India-Pakistan and India-Bangladesh borders.
    *   Suman Kumari is recognized as the first woman sniper of the BSF.

    *   Singapore announced the implementation of the world's first green fuel levy.

*   **"Walk to Mars" Marathon:**
    *   Organized by the UAE, this initiative aims for participants to collectively cover the distance of 5.4 crore km (Earth to Mars) through walking, cycling, or running.

**Health & Environment**

*   **World Diabetes Day 2025:**
    *   Celebrated on November 14 (coinciding with Children's Day in India).
    *   Commemorates the birthday of Frederick Banting, a co-discoverer of insulin.
    *   Insulin, discovered by Best and Banting in 1922, is a hormone that regulates blood sugar and contains zinc, secreted by the beta cells of the pancreas.
*   **Salumarada Thimmakka:**
    *   A renowned environmentalist from Karnataka, passed away.
    *   She was famous for planting numerous banyan trees ("Salumarada" means "rows of trees").
    *   Awarded the Padma Shri in 2019.
