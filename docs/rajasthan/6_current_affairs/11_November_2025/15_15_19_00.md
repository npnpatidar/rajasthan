**Current Affairs Highlights**

*   **Morel Dam in Focus due to Chemical Waste:**
    *   Asia's largest unpaved dam, Morel Dam, located in Sawai Madhopur and Dausa, on the Morel River (a tributary of Banas River).
    *   Suffering from pollution due to chemical waste from textile units in Jaipur (Sanganeri and Bagru print) and Dausa (Jaipur Kalins textile park).
    *   Pollution has led to foul smell and death of migratory birds, including Siberian Cranes, which visit the dam and Keoladeo Ghana Bird Sanctuary.
    *   **Related Dam Information:**
        *   Jawai Dam (Pali) on Jawai River, known as 'Marwar ka Amrit Sarovar'.
        *   Bisalpur Dam (Tonk) on Banas River.
        *   Panchna Dam (Karauli), a soil-built dam.
