**November 2025 Current Affairs**

*   **Defence & International Relations**
    *   India and the USA signed a historic 10-year defense framework in Kuala Lumpur.
    *   India officially ceased operations at Airbase in Tajikistan.
    *   APEC Summit 2025 was held in South Korea.
    *   Germany provided the Patriot air defence system to Ukraine.
    *   Kazakhstan announced its intention to join the Abraham Accords.

    *   The 6th edition of the "Vinbax 25" military exercise was conducted between India and Vietnam.
    *   The "Garud 2025" exercise was conducted between India and France.
    *   Hanimaadhoo International Airport in Maldives was inaugurated with Indian assistance.

*   **Space & Technology**
    *   Sunrise OSOWOG GCC and SIDS solar platform panels were unveiled in New Delhi.
    *   ISRO launched CMS-03, India's heaviest communication satellite.
    *   China successfully converted thorium to uranium fuel for the first time globally.
    *   ISRO announced plans to launch Mangalyaan 2 by 2030.
    *   Google launched Project Skynets to test AI data centers in space.
    *   USA tested the Minuteman III ICBM.

    *   India's first Quantum Diamond Microscope was developed by IIT Bombay.
    *   ISRO tested the main parachute of the Gaganyaan Crew Module in Babina Field Firing Range, Uttar Pradesh.

*   **Economy & Business**
