**Rajasthan Current Affairs: November 26, 2025**

**Rural Development and Panchayati Raj Department Notification**

*   The Rural Development and Panchayati Raj Department issued a notification.
*   85 new Panchayat Samitis were formed.
*   3440 new Gram Panchayats were formed.
*   **Current Status (as of notification):**
    *   Zila Pramukhs: 41
    *   Panchayat Samitis: 437
    *   Gram Panchayats: 14781
*   **Contextual Information on Panchayati Raj:**
    *   Panchayati Raj Day: April 24 (first observed on April 24, 2010).
    *   Reason for April 24: 73rd Constitutional Amendment (1992) related to Panchayati Raj system was implemented in India on April 24, 1993.
    *   Implementation in Rajasthan: April 23, 1994.
    *   Inauguration of Panchayati Raj system in India: October 2, 1959, in Bagdari village, Nagaur, by Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru.

**Mukhyamantri Mangala Pashu Bima Yojana**

*   **Announcement:** Budget 2024-25.
*   **Initial Aim:** Free insurance for 21 lakh livestock.
*   **Launch:** December 13, 2024, at Kisan Sammelan in Kay, Ajmer.
*   **Budget 2025-26 Updates:**
    *   Number of insured livestock doubled to 42 lakh.
    *   Insurance is free and on a "first come, first served" basis.
*   **Insurance Details:**
    *   A Jan Aadhaar cardholder can insure a maximum of two milch animals.
    *   **Cow:** 3-12 years old, ₹3000 per kg of milk, maximum ₹40,000.
    *   **Buffalo:** 4-12 years old, ₹4000 per kg of milk, maximum ₹40,000.
    *   **Sheep & Goat:** 1-6 years old (female), ₹400 per unit (10 animals).
    *   **Camel:** 2-15 years old, ₹40,000.

**Ancient Civilization Discoveries**

*   **Jhunjhunu:** Evidence of a 4500-year-old civilization found in Bansiyal, Jhunjhunu district.
    *   Bansiyal is part of the Khetri-Bansiyal Leopard Reserve.
    *   **Researchers:** Dr. Isha Prasad and Dr. Shweta Sinha.
    *   **Discoveries:** Lime powder walls, copper rings, bone tools, and ornaments.
*   **Other locations:** Similar evidence of a 4500-year-old civilization also found in Behaj village (Deeg) and Ratariya ki Dairy (Jaisalmer).

**SMAC Center Award 2025**

*   Professor Neelima Gupta of Rajasthan University's Chemistry Department was awarded the SMAC Center Award 2025.
*   The award was presented at the 7th National Workshop on Material Chemistry held in Mumbai.

**Legal Awareness and Education Campaigns**

*   **"Nyay Tak" Campaign:**
    *   Launched by the District Legal Services Authority, Jaipur.
    *   Part of the "Kanoon Connect" initiative, conducted in schools.
    *   Aims to raise legal awareness and provide free legal aid.
*   **"Karmabhoomi Se Matrubhoomi Abhiyan" (Water Conservation):**
    *   Launched on January 15, 2025, under the "Catch the Rain" campaign.
    *   Involves collaboration with philanthropists and Non-Resident Rajasthanis for water conservation.
    *   Implemented in six districts: Jhunjhunu, Jaipur, Bhilwara, Jodhpur, Pali, and Sirohi.
*   **"Shikshit Rajasthan Abhiyan" (Educated Rajasthan Campaign):**
    *   Launched on March 29, 2025, from a Youth Conference in Kota.
    *   Aims to develop reading skills among students from classes 3 to 8.
*   **"Prakhar Rajasthan 2.0":**
    *   A 90-day program under the "Shikshit Rajasthan Abhiyan."
    *   Launched on Teacher's Day (September 5, 2025) and concluded on December 5, 2025.
    *   Focused on enhancing reading skills in students from classes 3 to 8.

**Jaigarh Heritage Festival**

*   The Jaigarh Heritage Festival will be held from December 6-7, 2025.
*   **Context:** Jaigarh Fort, located on the Eagle Hills in Jaipur, is also known as Chil-ka-Teela or Sankat Mochan Durg. It once housed a cannon foundry where the Jai Baan cannon (Asia's largest wheeled cannon) was cast.

**Para National Swimming Championship**
