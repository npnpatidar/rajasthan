**National Education Policy 2020 (NEP 2020) - Curriculum Changes**
*   **Rajasthan's Primary Classes:** The curriculum will now include the life story of the folk deity Tejaji.
*   **Key Provisions of NEP 2020:**
    *   **School Structure:** 5+3+3+4 (covering ages 3-18 years; 3 years pre-school, 2 years primary, followed by 3 years, 3 years, and 4 years).
    *   **Student-Teacher Ratio:** Aim for less than 30:1 in general areas, and less than 25:1 in economically and socially disadvantaged areas.
    *   **Gross Enrollment Ratio:** Target 100% GER by 2030.
    *   **Medium of Instruction:** Up to 5th grade, the medium should be local language, regional language, or mother tongue.
    *   **Research:** Establishment of a National Research Foundation to promote research.
    *   **Education Expenditure:** 6% of GDP to be allocated to education.
    *   **Vocational Education:** Introduction of vocational education from Class 6.
    *   **Assessment:** Establishment of a National Assessment Center (PARAKH).
    *   **M.Phil. Program:** Abolished.
    *   **Higher Education Exit Options:** Certificate after 1 year, Diploma after 2 years, Degree after 3 years.
    *   **Teacher Qualification:** By 2030, the minimum qualification for teachers will be a B.Ed. degree.
    *   Focus on holistic development, critical thinking, and connecting education to local context and culture, rather than rote learning.
