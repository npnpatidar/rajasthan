### International Relations & Defense

*   **Joint Military Exercise:**
    *   India-Nepal joint military exercise "Surya Kiran 19" commenced in Pithoragarh, Uttarakhand.
    *   This marks the 19th edition of the exercise.
    *   India's Assam Regiment participated.
    *   Nepal's capital is Kathmandu, also the headquarters of SAARC.
    *   Five Indian states border Nepal: Uttarakhand, Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, West Bengal, and Sikkim.
    *   Disputes with Nepal include territories like Kalapani and Lipulekh in Uttarakhand.
    *   The "Janji Protest" occurred in Nepal, leading to the departure of former PM K.P. Sharma Oli, with Sushila Karki currently serving as PM.
    *   "Sampriti" and "Bongosagar" are exercises with Bangladesh, and "Mitra Shakti" is with Sri Lanka.
    *   Operation Pawan (1987), launched by India under PM Rajiv Gandhi, aimed to establish peace in Sri Lanka and counter the LTTE. After 38 years, the Indian Army will officially honor the approximately 1171 martyrs of this operation.
    *   Other Indian military operations:
        *   **Operation Cactus:** Maldivian coup attempt.
        *   **Operation Trishul:** 1971, targeting Karachi Port by the Indian Navy.
        *   **Operation Brahma:** Aid to Myanmar.
        *   **Operation Sindhu:** Evacuation of Indians from Iran.
    *   The Indian Army conducted the "Amog Fury Avni" exercise in Rajasthan.
    *   India and Greece navies conducted their first bilateral maritime exercise in the Mediterranean Sea.
    *   Saudi Arabia entered a NATO-based "attack on one is an attack on all" agreement with Pakistan.

*   **Space & Technology:**
    *   Oman launched its first National Communication Satellite, "OmanSat First," in partnership with Airbus, to establish high-speed and secure communication using K-A band technology.
    *   NASA-ISRO Synthetic Aperture Radar (NISAR) was launched using GSLV 6.
    *   Jupiter, the world's fourth-fastest supercomputer and Europe's fastest, was launched in Germany.

### Economy & Infrastructure

*   **Blue Port Development:** India and FAO agreed to establish world-class Blue Ports for sustainable fisheries at Sojhakau, Banakavara, and Karaikal.
*   **Strategic Petroleum Reserve:** Mega Engineering will establish India's first private sector strategic petroleum reserve in Karnataka.
*   **Aviation Services:** PM Modi inaugurated the Safran Aircraft Engine Services India Facility in Hyderabad, which will provide Maintenance, Repair, and Overall (MRO) services for aircraft engines (LEAP engines) of Airbus and Boeing. Safran is a French aviation company.
*   **Largest Cruise Terminal:** PM Modi inaugurated India's largest cruise terminal in Mumbai.
*   **Financial Services:** The RBI approved PhonePe to operate as an online payment aggregator.
*   **Insurance Portal:** Bima Sugam Portal was launched in Hyderabad, where the IRDAI headquarters is located.
*   **New Security Regulator:** The central government launched CISF as the new security regulator for 250 Indian ports.
*   **Defence Manufacturing:** India's first foreign (outside India) defense manufacturing plant under the "Atmanirbhar Bharat" initiative was established in Morocco.
*   **Pellet Plant:** Union Minister Pralhad Joshi inaugurated a pellet plant in Rewari, Haryana.

(She was previously the first female Governor of Jharkhand and an MLA from Rairangpur, Odisha).

*   **Zero Maternal Mortality:** Puducherry became the first Union Territory to achieve a zero maternal mortality rate.
*   **e-Governance Conference:** The 28th National Conference on e-Governance was held in Visakhapatnam.
*   **Farmers' Scheme:** The Andhra Pradesh government launched the "Raithanna Meesam Yojana" to empower farmers by providing irrigation facilities, promoting technology, market-driven crop production, and flexible farming.
*   **New Districts in Andhra Pradesh:** Three new districts – Polavaram, Markapuram, and Madanapalle – were announced in Andhra Pradesh, increasing the total to 29.
