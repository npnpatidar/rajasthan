### Census 2011 Data

*   **Census Milestones:**
    *   First census: 1872 (under Viceroy Mayo).
    *   First decennial (scientific) census: 1881 (under Viceroy Ripon).
    *   First Census Commissioner of India: W.C. Plowden.
    *   2011 census: 15th overall, 7th since independence (1951).
    *   "Great Divide Decade" (महाविभाजक दशक): 1911-1921, marked by a population decrease due to famines and epidemics, leading to 1921 being called the "Great Divide Year".
*   **2011 Census Specifics:**
    *   Census Commissioner: C. Chandramouli.
    *   Motto: "Our Census, Our Future" (हमारी जनगणना हमारा भविष्य).
    *   Total Population: 121.09 crore (second globally at the time, now first).
    *   Decadal Growth Rate (2001-2011): 17.7%.
    *   Gender Distribution: Male 51.47%, Female 48.53%.
*   **Area-wise States/UTs:**
    *   Largest State (area): Rajasthan.
    *   Smallest State (area): Goa.
    *   Largest Union Territory (area): Ladakh.
    *   Smallest Union Territory (area): Lakshadweep.
*   **Population-wise States:**
    *   Maximum Population: Uttar Pradesh (16.51% of India's population).
    *   Minimum Population: Sikkim (0.05%).
*   **Decadal Growth Rate (States):**
    *   Maximum Decadal Growth: Meghalaya (27.9%).
    *   Minimum Decadal Growth (negative): Nagaland (-0.6%).
*   **Population Density:**
    *   India's Density: 382 persons/sq km.
    *   Maximum Density (State): Bihar (1106 persons/sq km).
    *   Minimum Density (State): Arunachal Pradesh (17 persons/sq km).
    *   Maximum Density (UT): Delhi.
    *   Minimum Density (UT): Lakshadweep.
*   **Sex Ratio:**
    *   India's Sex Ratio: 943 females/1000 males.
    *   Maximum Sex Ratio (State): Kerala (1084 females/1000 males).
    *   Minimum Sex Ratio (State): Haryana (879 females/1000 males).
    *   Child Sex Ratio (0-6 years):
        *   Top: Arunachal Pradesh (972).
        *   Minimum: Haryana (834).
*   **Literacy Rate:**
    *   India's Literacy Rate: 73%.
    *   Male Literacy: 80.09%, Female Literacy: 64.60%.
    *   Maximum Literacy (State): Kerala (94%).
    *   Minimum Literacy (State): Bihar (61.80%).
*   **Rural/Urban Population:**
    *   Maximum Rural Population (percentage): Himachal Pradesh (90%).
    *   Maximum Urban Population (percentage): Goa (62.2%).
    *   Maximum Urban Population (by numbers): Maharashtra (5.08 crore).
*   **SC/ST Population:**
    *   Maximum Scheduled Caste (SC) Population (by numbers): Uttar Pradesh (approx. 4.25 crore).
    *   Maximum SC Population (by percentage): Punjab (31.9%).
    *   Maximum Scheduled Tribe (ST) Population (by numbers): Madhya Pradesh (approx. 1.5 crore).
    *   Maximum ST Population (by percentage): Mizoram (94%).

### Current Affairs

**International Relations & Defense**

*   **AusINDEX (Ocinidex):** Joint naval exercise between India (INS Sahyadri) and Australia in the North Pacific Ocean. It is a biennial exercise.
*   **Malabar Exercise:** Hosted in Guam, involved India, Australia, USA, and Japan.
*   **Garud Shakti & Samudra Shakti:** Joint exercises between India and Indonesia.
*   **Konkan Exercise & Indradhanush Exercise:** Joint exercises between India and England.
*   **US-Russian Crew to ISS:** Three astronauts launched for an 8-month mission to the International Space Station (ISS) from Baikonur, Kazakhstan, using the Soyuz MS-28 rocket.
    *   Kazakhstan is the world's largest landlocked country, with Astana as its capital. It is known for Balkhash Lake and uranium reserves.
    *   Indian astronaut Shubhanshu Shukla (from Lucknow) previously visited the ISS via the Axiom 04 mission.
*   **US-Russia Peace Agreement:** Mediated by former US President Trump, an historic peace agreement was reached between Azerbaijan and Armenia regarding the Nagorno-Karabakh region.
*   **G20 Summit 2026:** Former US President Trump stated that South Africa would not be invited to the 2026 G20 Summit in Miami, citing South Africa's conduct at the 2025 G20 Summit in Johannesburg.
*   **SCO Summit 2025:** The 25th Shanghai Cooperation Organisation (SCO) Summit was held in Tianjin, China.
*   **International Solar Alliance (ISA):** Moldova became the 107th member.

**Science & Technology**

*   **Divya Drishti:** Indian Army's high-altitude technical demonstration exercise conducted in Sikkim.
*   **Make in India Green Hydrogen Plant:** India's first 1 MW green hydrogen plant started in Kandla, Gujarat.
*   **SRY Gene Technique:** A technique used for sex determination.
*   **International Astronomical Union (IAU) Mars Map:** IAU included seven new names of Indian origin on the map of Mars (the Red Planet).
    *   Five names from Kerala: Periyar, Bekal, Varkala, Thumba, Valiamala.
    *   Two names after M.S. Krishnan (first head of the Geological Survey of India): a crater and a plain.
*   **Dhruv Space Mission:** Dhruv Space launched its first commercial mission "Leaf First" using a Falcon 9 rocket.
*   **AI Data Center:** Reliance Industries invested $11 billion to develop an AI Data Center in Visakhapatnam.
*   **Animal Stem Cell Bio-bank & Lab:** India's first state-of-the-art facility inaugurated in Hyderabad by Union Minister Jitendra Singh.
*   **Telecommunications:** Center for Development of Telematics (C-DOT) signed an agreement with IIT Roorkee.

**Environment & Geography**

*   **Volcanic Eruptions:**
    *   Klyuchevskaya Sopka volcano erupted in Kamchatka Peninsula, Russia.
    *   A volcano erupted in Ethiopia after 12,000 years.
*   **Aravalli Hills Mining:** The Supreme Court approved a new definition for Aravalli Hills to curb illegal mining.
    *   New Definition: Any landform in the Aravalli region that is 100 meters or more higher than the local relief, including its slopes, will be considered part of the Aravalli Hills.
    *   The Aravalli range extends from Gujarat to Raisina Hills in Delhi, with Guru Shikhar (Mount Abu, Rajasthan) as its highest peak.
*   **Cyclone Ditwah:** Hit Sri Lanka, causing floods. Named by Yemen, meaning "Lagoon" (shallow). Expected to affect coastal regions of India (Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh, Kerala) around November 30.
    *   Sri Lanka's President: Anura Kumara Dissanayake.
    *   Cyclones are known as "Willy-Willies" in Australia.
*   **Uttarakhand Floods:** Flash floods occurred in the Kheerganga river in Uttarkashi district, Uttarakhand, due to heavy rainfall.

**National Initiatives & Policies**

*   **Operation Crystal Fortes:** Launched by the Narcotics Control Bureau (NCB) and Delhi Police against drug peddlers (specifically methamphetamine/ice drug) in Delhi.
*   **Operation Medmax:** An initiative to expose the global drug network.
*   **"Apna Ghar" Initiative:** A nationwide initiative by the Petroleum Ministry for truck drivers.
*   **Gomti Nagar Railway Station:** Uttar Pradesh's first private railway station.
*   **Face-less GST Adjudication:** Kerala became the first state to implement a pilot project for face-less GST adjudication.
*   **Koti Gita Lekhan Yagya Campaign:** PM Modi participated in this campaign at Shri Puthige Math in Udupi, Karnataka. He also inaugurated the "Kanaka Kidhi Window" (historically opened by Lord Krishna for his devotee Kanakadas).
*   **Lord Ram Statue in Goa:** PM Modi unveiled the world's tallest statue of Lord Ram in Goa. It is a 77-foot bronze statue, sculpted by Ram Sutar (who also designed the Statue of Unity).
*   **Dr. B.R. Ambedkar Statue:** A statue of Dr. B.R. Ambedkar was unveiled at the UNESCO Headquarters in Paris on November 26 (Constitution Day).
*   **Mukhyamantri Nijut Moina Yojana:** Launched in Assam to promote higher education for girls.
*   **SC Reservation Bill:** The Lok Sabha passed a bill to provide Scheduled Caste (SC) reservation in the Goa state assembly.
*   **DGP-IGP Conference 2025:** Will be held in Chhattisgarh.
*   **Rare Earth Magnet Manufacturing:** The Central Cabinet approved ₹7280 crore for rare earth magnet manufacturing.
