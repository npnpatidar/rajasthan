**Current Affairs - November 7, 2025**

**Awards and Recognition**
*   **World Rabies Day Award:** Awarded to Dr. Sanjay Singh by the Global Alliance for Rabies Control.
    *   Rabies is a virus-borne disease, primarily spread through bites from infected animals like dogs and bats.
    *   The rabies vaccine was discovered by Louis Pasteur in 1885.
    *   World Rabies Day is observed annually on September 28.
*   **National Lalit Kala Akademi Award:** Conferred upon Dr. Kanupriya and Dr. Giriraj Sharma.
*   **Maharshi Ved Vyas Award:** Awarded to Gulab Kothiyari at an event in Pune.

**International Events & Rajasthan's Participation**
*   **World Travel Market 2025 (London):** The Rajasthan Tourism Pavilion was inaugurated by Diya Kumari.
    *   Diya Kumari holds portfolios as Rajasthan's Tourism Minister, Finance Minister, and Minister for Women and Child Development.
    *   **Rajasthan Tourism Data:**
        *   Peak Tourist Season: November to March.
        *   Top Foreign Tourist Origin Countries: USA, UK, France.
        *   City with most Domestic Tourists: Sikar.
        *   City with most Foreign Tourists: Jaipur.

**Fairs and Festivals**
*   **Chandrabhaga Cattle Fair:** Held in Jhalrapatan, Jhalawar, on Kartik Purnima.
    *   **Jhalrapatan (City of Bells):**
        *   Features a Surya Temple, also known as Saisliyon ka Mandir, Padnabh Mandir, and Bada Mandir, modeled after the Konark Sun Temple.
        *   Home to the Shitaleshwar Mahadev Temple, the oldest dated temple.
        *   Unique feature: A temple featuring Surya Bhagwan (Sun God) depicted wearing shoes.
*   **Other Notable Fairs:**
    *   **Kolayat, Bikaner:** Kapil Muni Ashram and Kolayat Lake; hosts a fair on Kartik Purnima.
    *   **Chaksu, Jaipur:** Hosts the Shitala Mata Fair (Shitala Mata is the goddess of smallpox, depicted with a donkey as her mount).
    *   **Shivvad, Sawai Madhopur:** Location of a prominent Lord Shiva Temple.
*   **65th Lakh Anukoot Mahotsav:** Organized at Shri Khole Ke Hanuman Ji in Jaipur.

**Agriculture and Economy**
*   **Honey Production in Rajasthan:**
    *   Contributes approximately 9% to India's total honey production.
    *   Ranks 5th among states in honey production.
    *   "Sweet Revolution" (मीठी क्रांति) is the term associated with honey production.
    *   Major production regions: Northern and Eastern Rajasthan.
    *   **Honey FPO (Farmer Producer Organization):** Established in Bharatpur.
    *   **One District One Product Scheme:** Honey is the designated agricultural product for Bharatpur district.
    *   **Budget 2025-26:** Announcement for a "Centre of Excellence for Honey Bee Keeping" in Bharatpur.
    *   **Established Centre:** A "Centre of Excellence for Honey" has been established in Devadawas, Tonk.
*   **High-tech Banana Cultivation:** Initiated for the first time in Rajasthan in Udaipur.
*   **Limestone Block Auction:** Rajasthan's first-ever auction of limestone blocks will take place in Bhilwara.
