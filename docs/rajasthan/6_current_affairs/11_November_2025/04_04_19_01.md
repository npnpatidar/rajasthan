### Rajasthan Shops and Commercial Establishments Ordinance 2025

*   The Rajasthan Shops and Commercial Establishments Ordinance 2025 was recently approved by CM Bhajanlal Sharma.
*   The minimum age for employment in shops/commercial establishments has been raised to 14 years.
*   Adolescents aged 14-18 years are prohibited from working at night.
*   The maximum daily working hours for laborers have been set at 10 hours.
*   Maximum overtime allowed is 144 hours over a period of three months.

### Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel's 150th Birth Anniversary

*   A state-level Unity March (Rajya Stariya Unity March) was organized in Jaipur on October 31, 2025, to commemorate the 150th birth anniversary of Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel.
*   Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, known as the "Iron Man of India," was India's first Home Minister and the Chairman of the Riyasati Vibhag (States Department), established on July 5, 1947.
*   Born on October 31, 1875, in Gujarat, his birthday is celebrated as National Unity Day (Rashtriya Ekta Diwas).
*   The Statue of Unity, a tribute to Sardar Patel, is located in Gujarat, stands 182 meters tall, and was inaugurated on October 31, 2018. Its architect is Ram Sutar.

*   **Other notable statues in Rajasthan:**
    *   **Vishwas Swaroopam (Statue of Belief):** Located in Nathdwara, Rajsamand, it is the world's tallest Shiva statue, measuring 369 feet. Its architect is Naresh Kumawat.
    *   **Rajmata (ST-2) Tigress Statue:** Situated in Sariska Tiger Reserve.
    *   **Tigress Machhali Statue:** Located in Ranthambore National Park.
    *   **Statue of Truth (Maharishi Dayanand Saraswati):** Found in Ajmer.
    *   **Statue of Peace:** Located in Pali.

### Country's Largest Oran (Sacred Grove)

*   The Bhadariya Oran in Jaisalmer is recognized as the country's largest sacred grove.
*   **Oran definition:** A sacred grove and biodiversity conservation area (Pavitra Upvan evam Jaiv Vividhta Sanrakshan Kshetra), particularly in Western Rajasthan.
*   It has been a topic of discussion due to ongoing land allocation to solar energy companies in Western Rajasthan, which has led to local protests.
*   An Oran Protection Committee has been formed for its conservation, chaired by Jitendra Kumar.

*   **Other Orans in Rajasthan:**
    *   Degrai Mata Oran: Jaisalmer.
    *   Deshnok Oran: Bikaner.
    *   Viratra Mata Oran: Jaisalmer.

*   **Related Current Affairs:**
    *   **Deomali (Beawar):** Awarded the "Best Tourism Village."
    *   **Khichan and Menar:** Included in Ramsar Sites on June 4, 2025.
