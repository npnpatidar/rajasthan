**Nobel Prizes 2025**

*   **General Information:**
    *   Nobel Prize established in 1901 based on the will of Alfred Nobel (inventor of dynamite).
    *   Awarded in six fields. The Economics Prize was established later in 1968 by Sweden's Sveriges Riksbank (also known as the Riksbank Award) and first given in 1969.
    *   All Nobel Prizes are awarded in Stockholm, Sweden, except the Peace Prize, which is awarded in Oslo, Norway.
    *   Announcements are made earlier, but the prizes are formally awarded on December 10th, marking Alfred Nobel's death anniversary.

*   **Winners by Category (2025):**
    *   **Physiology/Medicine:**
        *   **Recipients:** Mary Brunkov, Friend Remmesdahl, Simon Sakaguchi.
        *   **Awarded for:** Discovery of immune system tolerance.
        *   **First Recipient (1901):** Adolf von Behring.
    *   **Physics:**
        *   **Recipients:** Michelle S. Devereau, John Clauser, John M. Martinez.
        *   **Awarded for:** Microscopic Quantum Mechanical Tunnelling in electric circuits.
        *   **First Recipient (1901):** Wilhelm Conrad Röntgen (for X-ray discovery).
    *   **Chemistry:**
        *   **Recipients:** Richard Robson, Omar Yaghi, Susumu Kitagawa.
        *   **Awarded for:** Work on Metal-Organic Frameworks (MOFs).
        *   **First Recipient (1901):** Jacobus Henricus van 't Hoff.
    *   **Literature:**
        *   **Recipient:** Laszlo Krasznahorkai (Hungarian author).
        *   **Awarded for:** His captivating and visionary works.
        *   **First Recipient (1901):** Sully Prudhomme.
    *   **Peace:**
        *   **Recipient:** Maria Corina Machado (Venezuelan opposition leader).
        *   **First Recipients (1901):** Henry Dunant (founder of Red Cross) and Frédéric Passy.
    *   **Economics:**
        *   **Recipients:** Joel Mokyr, Philippe Aghion, Peter Howitt.
        *   **Awarded for:** Identifying necessary conditions for sustainable development through technological progress and the theory of sustainable development through creative destruction.
        *   **First Recipients (1969):** Ragnar Frisch and Jan Tinbergen (from the Netherlands).

**US Declares Saudi Arabia a Major Non-NATO Ally (MNNA)**
*   **NATO (North Atlantic Treaty Organization):** Established in 1949, with 32 members (latest being Sweden), and headquartered in Brussels, Belgium.
*   **MNNA (Major Non-NATO Associate Member):** A designation given by the US to non-NATO countries with strategic working relationships.
*   **Declaration:** The US formally designated Saudi Arabia as an MNNA, which is expected to facilitate arms deals and enhance cooperation.
*   **Saudi Arabia:** Capital is Riyadh, currency is Riyal, and it is home to deserts like Rub' al Khali and Nafud.

**VIRSA 1001 Gene Therapy**
*   **Type:** CRISPR gene therapy, designed to replace faulty genes with healthy ones to treat genetic diseases.
*   **Application:** Specifically for the treatment of Sickle Cell Anemia, a genetic disorder prevalent in tribal areas where red blood cells become sickle-shaped.
*   **Naming:** Named after Bhagwan Birsa Munda, a revered tribal leader, whose 150th birth anniversary was on November 15.
*   **India's Elimination Target:** India aims to eliminate Sickle Cell Anemia by 2047.
