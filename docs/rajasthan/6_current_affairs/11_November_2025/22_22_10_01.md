### International Organizations & Disputes

*   **Colombo Security Conclave:**
    *   Seychelles became the 6th new member.
    *   Existing members: India, Maldives, Sri Lanka, Bangladesh, Mauritius.
    *   The 7th meeting of the Conclave was hosted in New Delhi.
    *   Objective: To enhance maritime security in the Indian Ocean.
    *   Capital of Seychelles: Victoria.
*   **Latest Members in Other Organizations:**
    *   NATO: Sweden (32nd member).
    *   SCO: Belarus (10th member).
    *   G20: African Union.
    *   ASEAN: Timor-Leste (11th member).
*   **Senkaku Islands Dispute:**
    *   Tension increased between China and Japan over the Senkaku Islands.
    *   The islands are currently under Japanese control, but China claims them.
    *   The recent tension escalated after Japan's Prime Minister Sanae Taichi expressed support for Taiwan.
    *   Google plans to establish its largest AI hardware engineering center in Taiwan.
    *   **Other Territorial Disputes/Regions:**
        *   Sakhalin Island: Disputed between Russia and Japan.
        *   Nine-Dash Line: China's claim in the South China Sea.
        *   Kamchatka Peninsula: Located in Russia.
*   **Basmati Rice GI Protection:**
    *   India's Basmati rice is facing global legal challenges regarding its Geographical Indication (GI) protection.
    *   The Agricultural and Processed Food Products Export Development Authority (APEDA) is India's exporting body for agricultural products.
    *   High Courts in New Zealand and Kenya have refused to recognize India's GI tag and marketing rights for Basmati rice, potentially setting a precedent.
    *   India relies on the TRIPS (Trade-Related Aspects of Intellectual Property Rights) agreement under the World Trade Organization (WTO) for GI protection.
    *   WTO was established on January 1, 1995, by the Marrakesh Agreement, headquartered in Geneva, Switzerland. Its current head is Ngozi Okonjo-Iweala.

### Science & Technology

*   **First Private PSLV Rocket:**
    *   Developed by a consortium of Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL) and Larsen & Toubro (L&T).
    *   This is the first Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle (PSLV) built by a private company in India.
    *   It is planned to launch the OceanSat satellite in early 2026.
    *   PSLV is a 4-stage rocket, using alternating solid and liquid fuel, operating on Newton's third law (action-reaction).
*   **Cassini Spacecraft:** NASA's Cassini mission found new evidence for life on Saturn's moon, Enceladus.
