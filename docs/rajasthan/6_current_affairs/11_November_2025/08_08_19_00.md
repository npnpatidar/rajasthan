### National Anthem: Vande Mataram

*   The National Anthem, Vande Mataram, is sung in 69 seconds.
*   It was authored by Bankim Chandra Chattopadhyay.
*   First sung by Rabindranath Tagore in 1896 at the Indian National Congress Calcutta session.
*   Adopted as the National Anthem on January 24, 1950.
*   The first Rajasthani poetic translation of Vande Mataram was prepared by Ikram Rajasthani.
*   **Bharatiya Sahitya Akademi Awards (2024, Rajasthani Language):**
    *   **Poonam Chand Godara:** Young Litterateur Award (Yuva Sahityakar) for "Antashree Aangane".
    *   **Bhogilal Patidar:** Children's Litterateur Award (Bal Sahityakar) for "Pankheruri Peeda".

### Road Accident Prevention in Rajasthan

*   **Campaign:** "Sarak Suraksha Abhiyan" (Road Safety Campaign) is being run from November 4 to 18, 2025.
*   **Road Safety Institute:** Established at HCM RIPA, Jaipur.
*   **Budget 2025-26 Initiatives:** Three National Highways to be made accident-free:
    *   NH-48 (Jaipur-Delhi)
    *   NH-21 (Jaipur-Dausa)
    *   NH-52 (Jaipur-Kota)
*   **"Sushma Surakshit Sadak Marg" Program:**
    *   Ran from August 9 to September 15, 2025.
    *   Aimed at providing road safety information to students.
    *   Covered 19 districts, starting from Jodhpur.
*   **"Mukhyamantri Shikshit Rajasthan Abhiyan":**
    *   Guidelines issued on March 29, 2025, at the Yuva Sammelan in Kota.
    *   Focuses on improving teaching in mathematics, English, and Hindi for students in classes 3 to 8.
*   **"Prakhar Rajasthan Abhiyan":**
    *   Runs from September 5 to December 5 (90-day program).
    *   Aims to develop reading skills among students.

### Census 2027 in Rajasthan

*   **16th Census of India:** To be conducted in 2027.
*   **Reference Dates:**
    *   March 1, 2027: For most states, including Rajasthan.
    *   October 1, 2026: For Ladakh, Jammu & Kashmir, Himachal Pradesh, and Uttarakhand.
*   **Pilot Project in Rajasthan:** The census is being tested in three locations:
    *   Jaipur: किशन पोल (Kishan Pol) area of Jaipur Municipal Corporation.
    *   Barmer: Entire Barmer tehsil and some urban areas.
    *   Dungarpur: गलियाकोट (Galiyakot) area.
*   **First Phase Full Testing:** Scheduled from November 10 to 30.
