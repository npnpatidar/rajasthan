**Nobel Prizes**

**Nobel Prize General Information:**
*   Nobel Prize established in 1901, following Alfred Nobel's will from 1895.
*   Economics prize added in 1968, first awarded in 1969, established by Sweden's Sveriges Riksbank (Riksbank Award).
*   All Nobel Prizes (except Peace) are awarded in Stockholm, Sweden.
*   The Peace Nobel Prize is awarded in Oslo, Norway.
*   Prizes are presented on December 10th, the anniversary of Alfred Nobel's death.
