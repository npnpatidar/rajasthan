### Media Lab and Community Radio Inauguration

*   **Event:** Inauguration of Media Lab and Radio Rajasthan 91.2 Community Radio.
*   **Location:** Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya Shekhawati University, Sikar.
*   **Inaugurated by:** Governor (as the Chancellor of the university).
*   **Related Sikar Facts:**
    *   Uranium reserves at Khandela hills and Rohil village.
    *   Ganeshwar Civilization.
    *   Baijnath Maharaj (Padma Shri 2025 recipient) hails from here.
    *   Khandela is famous for the Gota industry (Lappa-Zappi).
    *   Pyrite production.
    *   First Sainik Academy of the state: Rao Sekha Academy.
*   **Governor-Related Context:**
    *   Completed one year in office.
    *   Released the book 'Abhyudaya Ki Ore', inaugurated by Somati Devi.
*   **Other Radio Station:** Maharana Pratap University of Agriculture and Technology, Udaipur, also launched a radio station: MPUAT 90.4 FM (focused on agriculture).

*   **Location:** Jawahar Kala Kendra, Jaipur.
*   **Dates:** October 29-30, 2025.

*   **Historical Context (Mehrangarh Fort, Jodhpur):**
    *   Located on the Chiria Took hills.
    *   Notable cannons include Gazak, Nusrat, Shambhu Baan, Kilkil, Kadak, Bijli, and Nagfani.

### World's First Cattle Rearing Corridor

*   **Event:** Foundation stone laid for the world's first Gopalan (Cattle Rearing) Corridor.
*   **Location:** Nand Gaon, Sirohi.
*   **Initiated by:** Om Prakash Mathur.
*   **Date:** Kartik Shukla Ashtami.
*   **Related Sirohi Facts:**
    *   Mount Abu: Home to Mount Abu Sanctuary (known for wild fowls), Nakki Lake, Toad Rock, Nandi Rock, ISRO Infrared Center, Arbuda Mata Temple.
    *   Features the Uria Plateau and Abu Plateau.
*   **Government Initiatives for Cattle Rearing:**
    *   Budget 2025-26: ₹5,000 annual incentive to farmers for cultivation using bullocks.
    *   Gaushala grants: ₹50 per day for cows, ₹25 per day for calves.
