**Global Indices & Reports**

*   **World Intellectual Property Indicators Report:** India ranks 6th globally in patent filing (over 63,000 patents in a year); China is 1st. India ranks 4th in trademark filing and 7th in design filing.
*   India ranks 38th in the Global Innovation Index.

**International Relations & Initiatives**

*   **India-Nepal Rail Connectivity:** India and Nepal exchanged a Letter of Exchange to enhance rail connectivity via the Jogbani-Biratnagar corridor, aiming to boost freight movement and trade.
*   **Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) One-Stop Travel System:** The GCC approved a "One Stop Travel System" for movement between its six member countries (Saudi Arabia, Oman, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain, UAE), allowing a single check for migration, security, and customs.
*   **India-Central Asian Countries Cyber Exercise:** India and five Central Asian countries (Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, Turkmenistan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan) conducted a 3-day Strategic Cyber Exercise in New Delhi to enhance cyber security.
*   **Third International Ayurveda Conference:** Held in Sao Paulo, Brazil.
*   **Chess E-Sports World Cup:** Held in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, won by Magnus Carlsen (Norway).

**National Policies & Initiatives**

*   **Water Conservation Awards:** Telangana received the "Best Performing State" award under the 'Jal Sanchay, Jan Bhagidari' (Water Conservation, Public Participation) initiative, presented by President Droupadi Murmu. Maharashtra received Best State for Water Conservation in the 6th National Water Awards.
*   **Nasha Mukt Bharat Abhiyan (Drug-Free India Campaign):** Launched on August 15, 2020, this 5-year campaign will conclude in 2025 with a closing ceremony at Guru Nanak Dev University, Amritsar, Punjab.
*   India achieved the target of 20% ethanol blending in petrol five years ahead of schedule.
*   **PESA Excellence Center:** Launched in Bhopal, focusing on the Panchayati Raj Extension to Scheduled Areas (PESA) Act, 1996.
*   **First Hindi MBBS College:** To be started in Madhya Pradesh.
*   **Apna Ghar Initiative:** The Petroleum Ministry launched this nationwide initiative for truck drivers.
*   **CII Partnership Summit:** The 30th CII Partnership Summit was held in Visakhapatnam.
