**Key Events in Indian History (1901-1950)**

*   **7 August 1905:** Boycott of foreign goods started at Kolkata's Town Hall in protest of Bengal partition.
*   **16 October 1905:** Bengal partition confirmed; celebrated as Raksha Bandhan Day.
*   **1907:** Surat Session of Congress; partition of moderate and extremist factions (Garama Dal and Narama Dal).
*   **1909:** Morley-Minto Act introduced communal electorate system.
*   **9 January 1915:** Mahatma Gandhi returned to India; celebrated as Pravasi Bharatiya Divas.
*   **1916:** Lucknow Session of Congress; pact between Congress and Muslim League; reunion of moderate and extremist factions.
*   **1916:** Establishment of Banaras Hindu University (BHU); Home Rule League Movement started.
*   **1917:** Calcutta Session of Congress, Annie Besant became first female Congress president.
*   **1917:** Champaran Satyagraha in Bihar, Mahatma Gandhi's first participation, against indigo farming and Tinkathia system.
*   **1919:** Rowlatt Act (no appeal, no argument, no lawyer).
*   **1919:** Montagu-Chelmsford Reforms introduced dual system (dyarchy) in provinces.
*   **13 April 1919:** Jallianwala Bagh massacre in Amritsar.
*   **1920:** Non-Cooperation Movement began; Mahatma Gandhi renounced his Kaiser-e-Hind title.
*   **February 1922:** Chauri Chaura incident in Gorakhpur, UP; led to the end of the Non-Cooperation Movement.
*   **1923:** Swaraj Party founded by Chittaranjan Das and Motilal Nehru.
*   **1924:** Belgaum Session of Congress, presided by Mahatma Gandhi (first and only time).
*   **1925:** Kanpur Session of Congress, presided by Sarojini Naidu (first Indian female Congress president).
*   **9 August 1925:** Kakori Train Action (formerly Kakori Train Robbery).
*   **1927:** Simon Commission formed.
*   **1928:** Simon Commission arrived in India; Nehru Report presented.
*   **1929:** Lahore Session of Congress; demand for Purna Swaraj (complete self-rule).
*   **26 January 1930:** Declared Purna Swaraj Day.
*   **1930:** Civil Disobedience Movement began; Dandi March (Salt Satyagraha).
*   **1930:** First Round Table Conference (Congress did not participate).
*   **5 March 1931:** Gandhi-Irwin Pact signed.
*   **23 March 1931:** Bhagat Singh, Rajguru, and Sukhdev hanged.
*   **1931:** Second Round Table Conference; Mahatma Gandhi participated.
*   **1932:** Communal Award by Ramsay MacDonald; Poona Pact between Mahatma Gandhi and B.R. Ambedkar.
*   **1940:** August Offer; Individual Satyagraha Movement started (Vinoba Bhave first Satyagrahi).
*   **9 August 1942:** Quit India Movement began; "Do or Die" slogan; Operation Zero Hour initiated.
*   **March 1946:** Cabinet Mission formed to create the Constituent Assembly.
*   **9 December 1946:** First meeting of Constituent Assembly; Sachchidananda Sinha appointed interim president.
*   **13 December 1946:** Objective Resolution (Preamble) presented by Jawaharlal Nehru.
*   **22 January 1947:** Objective Resolution accepted.
*   **3 June 1947:** Mountbatten Plan announced partition of India and Pakistan.
*   **18 July 1947:** Indian Independence Act passed.
*   **22 July 1947:** National Flag adopted.
*   **26 November 1949:** Constitution of India completed and adopted.
*   **24 January 1950:** Constituent Assembly signed the Constitution; National Anthem and National Song adopted.
*   **26 January 1950:** Constitution of India fully came into effect.
*   **28 January 1950:** Supreme Court formed.
