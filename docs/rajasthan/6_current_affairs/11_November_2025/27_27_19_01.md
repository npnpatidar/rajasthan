**Rajasthan Legislative Assembly & Cultural Initiatives**
*   **Vande Mataram Gallery:**
    *   Inaugurated on November 26, 2025 (Constitution Day) in the Rajasthan Legislative Assembly, Jaipur.
    *   Opened by Vasudev Devnani.
    *   Rajasthan Legislative Assembly was the first in India to live-stream its proceedings on YouTube.
    *   Features a "Political Narrative Museum" (डिजिटल म्यूजियम: राजनीतिक आख्यान संग्रहालय), inaugurated in 2022.
    *   Utilizes the "National E-Vidhan App" (Neva App) for digitization.

**Guinness World Record**
*   **Smallest Sandalwood Spoon:** Artist Rinku Kumawat (Jaipur) created the smallest 1mm spoon from sandalwood, earning a Guinness World Record.
*   **Previous Works by Rinku Kumawat:**
    *   2mm idol of Lord Mahavir.
    *   4mm Shivling.
    *   10mm idol of Lord Ram.
    *   Lord Ram's idol carved on a pencil tip.
*   **Other Notable Artists:**
    *   Pawan Vyas (Bikaner): Turban artist, holds records for tying the largest, smallest, and fastest turbans.
    *   Pawan Jangid: Known as "Chandan ka Chtera" (sandalwood artist), specializes in sandalwood art.
    *   Vinod Jangid: Created a sandalwood sword gifted to PM Narendra Modi.

**Sports & Youth Initiatives**
*   **PIE School Olympics:**
    *   Jointly organized by Patrika in Education (PIE) and Rajasthan State Sports Council.
    *   Venue: Sawai Mansingh (SMS) Stadium, Jaipur.
    *   Dates: December 8-13, 2025.
    *   Categories: Students from classes 6-8 and 9-12, competing in sports like races, kabaddi, wrestling, and basketball.
    *   SMS Stadium also hosts the "Centre of Excellence for Sports" in Rajasthan.
*   **Rajasthan State Sports Council:**
    *   First President: V.J. Kanetkar.
    *   Current President: Neeraja K. Pawan.
    *   **Awards:**
        *   Maharana Pratap Award: Highest award for athletes in Rajasthan (₹5 lakh, established 1982-83).
        *   Guru Vashisht Award: Highest award for coaches in Rajasthan (₹5 lakh, established 1985-86).

**Public Health Campaign**
*   **State-level Sub-national Pulse Polio Campaign:**
    *   Launched on November 23, 2025.
    *   Inaugurated by Chief Minister Bhajan Lal Sharma.
    *   Slogan: "Do Boond Har Baar, Polio Par Jeet Rahe Barkarar" (Two drops every time, victory over polio remains).
    *   The National Pulse Polio Program began in India in 1995.
    *   Sub-national campaigns target high-risk areas like Rajasthan, conducting multiple vaccination drives annually.

**Agricultural Innovation**
*   **Batturam Gurjar (Karauli):** A farmer who successfully transformed barren land into fertile land for horticulture.
*   He initiated gardening by planting fig and Bael trees and now cultivates 56 types of plants.
*   **Karauli District Overview:**
    *   Uparnama (Nickname): "Dang ki Rani" (Queen of the Dang region).
    *   Panchana Dam: An earth-filled dam.
    *   Mandrayal Fort: Known as the "Key to Gwalior Fort."
    *   Rajgopal's Chhatri (cenotaph).
    *   Kaila Devi Temple: Built by Rajgopal, where "Languriya" songs are sung in devotion.
    *   Makardhwaj Temple: Noted as the first of its kind in India.
