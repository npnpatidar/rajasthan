**Rajasthan Current Affairs (November 17, 2025)**

**Appointments & Governance**

*   **New Chief Secretary:** V. Srinivas (1989 batch IAS officer, Rajasthan cadre) appointed as the 46th Chief Secretary of Rajasthan.
    *   The Chief Secretary is the state's highest administrative officer and the highest authority during crises.
    *   The Secretariat in Jaipur was established in 1949 based on the Shankar Rao Committee recommendations.
    *   **Historical Chief Secretaries:**
        *   First: K. Radhakrishnan.
        *   First Woman: Kushal Singh.
        *   Second Woman: Usha Sharma.
        *   Longest Tenure: B.S. Mehta.
        *   Shortest Tenure: H.M. Mathur.
        *   45th: Sudhansh Pant.
*   **Lalit K. Pawar:**
    *   Served as Chairman of RPSC.
    *   First Vice-Chancellor of Vishwakarma Skill University, Jaipur.
    *   Associated with the tourism slogan "Padharo Mhare Des".
    *   Headed the committee for the formation of 50 to 41 districts in Rajasthan.

**Pilgrimage & Social Schemes**

*   **Devsthan Vibhag Schemes:**
    *   **Sindhu Darshan Pilgrimage Scheme:**
        *   Provides financial assistance of 50% of travel cost or a maximum of ₹15,000 to pilgrims.
        *   Forms accepted until December 31.
        *   Launched on April 1, 2016.
        *   Benefits 200 pilgrims annually.
    *   **Senior Citizen Pilgrimage Scheme:**
        *   Launched in 2013 (train) and 2016 (air).
        *   Free travel.
        *   Budget 2025-26 announced an increase to 56,000 pilgrims (6,000 by air, 50,000 by rail).
    *   **Kailash Mansarovar Pilgrimage Scheme:**
        *   Launched on April 1, 2011.
        *   Benefits 100 pilgrims annually.
        *   Provides ₹1 lakh assistance per pilgrim.
    *   **Apna Dham Apna Kaam Scheme:** Launched in 2008 (promotes community participation in religious place development).
    *   **Kalash Moksha Yatra Scheme:** Launched in 2020 (for immersion of ashes, especially during COVID).
*   **Budget 2025-26:** Priests' honorarium increased to ₹7,500.

**Infrastructure & Development**

*   **Wax Museum:** A Wax Museum, modeled after Madame Tussauds in London, will be built in Jaisalmer.
    *   Jaipur already has a Wax Museum, with a recent announcement for a statue of Harmanpreet Kaur.
*   **North India's First Robotic Implant Hub:** To be established in Jaipur.
    *   Jaipur is being developed as a Medical Tourism Hub.
    *   SMS Hospital, Jaipur, already houses the state's first Robotic Lab.
    *   The announcement was made during "Oceanacon 2025," a dental medical festival in Jaipur, indicating a focus on robotic dental surgery.
*   **Jaisalmer Current Affairs:**
    *   **Mega Village:** Discovery of "flying dinosaurs."
    *   **Ratadiyara Dera:** Discovery of an ancient civilization.
    *   **Koodi Village:** Hosted the State-level Yoga Day on June 21, 2025.
    *   **Bhadariya:** Identified as the largest Oran (sacred grove).
    *   **Longewala:** Rajnath Singh inaugurated a Light and Sound show at the War Museum.
    *   **Bodana:** Site of a 2000 MW Solar Park.
