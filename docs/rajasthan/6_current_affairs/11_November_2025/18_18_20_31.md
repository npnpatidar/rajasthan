**Khushishala Program**
*   **Initiator:** Rajasthan State Council of Educational Research & Training (RSCERT).
    *   **Establishment:** November 11, 1978, in Udaipur, based on the Mehrotra Committee recommendations, initially named SIRT.
    *   **Renamed:** RSCERT on August 14, 2018.
    *   **Function:** Primarily designs curriculum for the education department.
*   **Khushishala Program:**
    *   **Target:** Students from Class 1 to 5.
    *   **Pilot Project Launch:** 2024, in 30 schools in Sirohi and Banswara.
    *   **Statewide Expansion:** Now implemented across Rajasthan.
    *   **Teacher Training:** 12,000 teachers to be trained.
    *   **Focus:** Mental and emotional well-being of children for a stress-free learning environment.
