### Rajasthan's Position in National Programs and Schemes

*   **PM Vishwakarma Yojana:** Rajasthan ranked **second** nationally.
    *   Launched: September 17, 2023.
    *   Objective: Provide training and financial aid for 18 traditional trades.
    *   Benefits: 40 hours basic + 120 hours advanced training with ₹500 daily stipend; ₹15,000 for toolkits; up to ₹3 lakh loan at 5% interest.

*   **Fit India Freedom Run Program:** Rajasthan secured the **first position**.
*   **PM KUSUM Yojana (Component B & C):** Rajasthan ranked **second** nationally.
*   **Overall:** Rajasthan achieved first place in 11 national programs and schemes.

*   **Launch Date:** December 4, 2024.
*   **Duration:** Until March 31, 2029.
*   **PUSH Framework:**
    *   **P (Promotes):** Focus on value addition and competitiveness.
    *   **U (Upgrades):** Enhance export-related infrastructure (logistics, connectivity).
    *   **S (Streamline):** Simplify export processes and reduce documentation.
    *   **H (Harness):** Promote digital technology.
