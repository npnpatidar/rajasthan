**Revolutionary Charu Chandra Bose Award Scheme**

*   **Objective:** Provide financial assistance to girls with physical disabilities, including those who are deaf, mute, or visually impaired, studying in government schools.
*   **Launch:** Session 2025-26.
*   **Origin:** Merged two previous schemes of the Balika Shiksha Foundation (established March 30, 1995):
    *   Sharik Akshamta Yukt Balika Hetu Arthik Sambhalta Yojana (started 2004-05).
    *   Mook Badhir wa Netraheen Balika Arthik Sambhalta Yojana (started 2005-06).
*   **Financial Assistance:**
    *   Classes 1-8: ₹2000 per year.
    *   Classes 9-12: ₹5000 per year.
*   **Application:** Information must be uploaded on the Shala Darpan Portal.

**State's First Zinc Park**

*   **Collaboration:** RIICO (Rajasthan State Industrial Development and Investment Corporation) and Hindustan Zinc Limited.
*   **Objective:** Establish an industrial park specializing in metals and manufacturing.
*   **Potential Locations:** Debari (Udaipur) and Chanderiya (Chittor), where Hindustan Zinc Limited has existing units.
*   **RIICO Background:**
    *   Established: 1969 as Rajasthan State Industrial and Mining Development Corporation (RSIMDC).

    *   Independent operations began: January 1, 1980.

**Civil Airport Land Transaction Restrictions**

*   **Location:** Uttarlai, Barmer.
*   **Action:** Civil Aviation Department issued a notification restricting the purchase and sale of approximately 150 bighas of land across three villages near the Barmer Airport.
*   **Reason:** Government permission is now required for land transactions due to the area's proximity to the international border, citing security concerns for the airport.
