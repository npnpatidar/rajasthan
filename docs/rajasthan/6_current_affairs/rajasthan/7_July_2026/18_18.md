### VBG Ramji Scheme (Employment Guarantee)

*   **Objective:** This program is an updated version of the NREGA and MGNREGA schemes, focusing on providing employment guarantees in rural areas.
*   **Employment Guarantee:** It provides a guarantee of 125 days of employment to individuals in rural areas who seek work.
    *   Wages are paid on a weekly basis.
    *   Payments are made via Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT).
*   **Delay Compensation:** In the event of a delay in wage payment, a penalty/interest of 0.05% of the wage amount is paid to the worker.
*   **Funding Pattern:** The financial burden is shared between the central and state governments in a 60:40 ratio.
*   **Agricultural Exemption:** During agricultural peak seasons (such as the harvesting of wheat, mustard, and millet), there is a 60-day period where no work is conducted under the scheme, allowing laborers to work in agricultural activities.
    *   The scheme was implemented in Rajasthan on July 1, 2026.
    *   The central government released a fund of ₹11,581 crore for one year for the scheme's implementation in the state.
*   **National Ranking:** Rajasthan holds the first position nationwide in labor employment under the VBG Ramji scheme. This was stated by Chief Minister Bhajanlal Sharma during the inauguration ceremony of the Gotan railway station.
