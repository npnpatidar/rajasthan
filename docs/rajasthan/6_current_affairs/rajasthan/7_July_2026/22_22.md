    *   **International Allocation:** 100 seats for students admitted to the world's top 50 institutions.
*   **Academic Year 2026-27:** The scheme will provide financial assistance/scholarships to 700 students.
    *   **International Institutions:** Applicants must have secured at least 75% marks in their last examination.
    *   **Domestic Institutions:** Applicants must have secured at least 65% marks in their last examination.
*   **Eligible Courses:** Undergraduate (UG), Postgraduate (PG), and PhD studies.
    *   **E1:** Annual income up to ₹8 lakh.
