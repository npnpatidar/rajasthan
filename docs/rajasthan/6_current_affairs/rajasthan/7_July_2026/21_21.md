### Rajasthan State Election Commission: Panchayat and Local Body Elections

*   **Election Schedule:** The Rajasthan State Election Commission has decided to conduct election processes for local bodies and panchayats between August 15, 2026, and November 15, 2026.
    *   **First Phase (Local Body Elections):** The announcement for local body elections is scheduled for August 15, 2026, with the notification to be issued on August 22, 2026.
    *   **Second Phase (Panchayat Elections):** The announcement for panchayat elections is scheduled for September 23, 2026.
*   **Constitutional/Legal Basis:** The Rajasthan State Election Commission was established in July 1994 under Article 243K of the Constitution.
    *   The Commission is responsible for conducting elections for local bodies (urban and rural) in the state.
    *   It is distinct from the Election Commission of India, which handles elections for the Lok Sabha, Vidhan Sabha, President, and Vice-President.
    *   **Chief Election Commissioner:** Rajeshwar Singh.
    *   **Election Officer:** Naveen Mahajan.
*   **OBC Commission Survey:** The state government cited the ongoing survey by the OBC Commission as a reason for the delay in holding elections. The survey is intended to determine seat allocations for different categories.

### Administrative Context and Motivation

*   **Election Year:** The years 2026, 2027, and 2028 are designated as election years.
*   **High Court Intervention:** The Rajasthan High Court intervened regarding the delay in conducting panchayat elections, directing the State Election Commission to ensure the timely holding of these polls as per the five-year mandate.

### Rajasthan Panchayat and Municipal Election Eligibility (Amendment)

*   **Eligibility Criteria:** The government has amended the rules to allow individuals with more than two children to contest elections in Rajasthan. Previously, individuals with more than two children were disqualified from contesting.
    *   **Rajasthan Panchayati Raj Act, 1994:** Amended under Section 19 to remove the restriction regarding the number of children.
    *   **Rajasthan Municipalities Act, 2009:** Amended under Section 24 to allow individuals with more than two children to contest municipal elections.

### Date Palm Plantation Expansion Scheme (Western Rajasthan)

*   **Implementing Scheme:** This initiative is being implemented under the **Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana**.

    *   To increase agricultural productivity (per hectare).
    *   To make farmers self-reliant.
    *   To promote agro-forestry through the plantation of date palms.
    *   **Orchard Establishment:** 75% subsidy on the cost of establishing a date palm orchard.

*   **Regional Focus:** The scheme is currently seeing the highest level of activity in the Bikaner district.
*   **Center of Excellence:** The Date Palm Center of Excellence (Center of Excellence for Date Palm) is located at Sagra Bhojka, Jaisalmer.
*   **Current Status in Bikaner:** The district is seeing an increase in date palm production, with 350 hectares of orchards already established.
*   **Benefits to Farmers:** Farmers are receiving government subsidies and favorable market prices for their produce.
