### Nobel Prize Overview and History

*   **Establishment:** The Nobel Prize was established in 1901, five years after the death of Alfred Nobel in 1896.
*   **Origin:** Alfred Nobel, the inventor of dynamite, prepared a will for the prize in 1895.
*   **Award Ceremony:** All Nobel Prizes are awarded in Stockholm, Sweden, except for the Peace Prize, which is awarded in Oslo, Norway.
*   **Award Date:** Prizes are awarded on December 10th, the death anniversary of Alfred Nobel.
*   **Categories:** There are six fields in total. Five fields (Literature, Peace, Physics, Chemistry, and Medicine) began in 1901.
*   **Economics Prize:** The Economics category was established later, in 1968. It is also known as the "Riksbank Award" because it was established by the Swedish bank, Sveriges Riksbank.
