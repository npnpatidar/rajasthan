### **16th BRICS Summit (Kazan, Russia)**

*   **Theme:** "Strengthening Multilateralism for Just Global Development and Security."
*   **Expanded Membership:** First summit featuring new members: Egypt, Ethiopia, Iran, and the United Arab Emirates.
*   **Kazan Declaration:**
    *   **Financial Reform:** Focus on "BRICS Pay" (a blockchain-based system) to reduce reliance on the US dollar and SWIFT.
    *   **Grain Exchange:** Proposal for "BRICS Clear," an independent platform for trading grain and other commodities.
    *   **Global Governance:** Called for the reform of the UN Security Council and Bretton Woods institutions (IMF/World Bank).
*   **India-China Bilateral:** PM Modi and President Xi Jinping held their first formal meeting in five years following the agreement on patrolling arrangements along the LAC in Eastern Ladakh.

### **National Affairs (India)**

*   **51st Chief Justice of India (CJI):** Justice Sanjiv Khanna appointed to succeed Justice D.Y. Chandrachud.
*   **Dadasaheb Phalke Award:** Conferred to veteran actor Mithun Chakraborty for his contribution to Indian cinema.
*   **Mission Mausam:** A government initiative launched to enhance India’s weather forecasting and climate management capabilities using AI and advanced satellites.
*   **Cyclone Dana:** A severe cyclonic storm that impacted the coasts of Odisha and West Bengal in October 2024.

### **International Relations & Defense**

*   **SCO Summit 2024:** Hosted in Islamabad, Pakistan. India represented by EAM S. Jaishankar; focus on regional security and the "Three Evils" (Terrorism, Separatism, Extremism).
*   **SIMBEX-2024:** The 31st edition of the Singapore-India Maritime Bilateral Exercise held at Visakhapatnam.
*   **NASA Europa Clipper:** Launched to study Jupiter’s moon, Europa, to determine if its subsurface ocean can support life.

### **Banking & Economy**

*   **RBI Monetary Policy (October 2024):**
    *   **Repo Rate:** Maintained at 6.5%.
    *   **Stance:** Shifted from "Withdrawal of Accommodation" to "Neutral," signaling potential future rate cuts.
*   **Unified Lending Interface (ULI):** RBI launched this platform to provide "frictionless credit" to farmers and MSMEs by integrating data from various states and central agencies.
